Who is Jolin Tsai:

Jolin Tsai is a famous World Music Singer. She was born on September 15, 1980 and her birthplace is Xinzhuang, Taiwan. Jolin is also well known as, Taiwanese singer and dancer who has won multiple Golden Melody Awards, including in the category for Best Mandarin Female Singer in 2007.

Jolin is originated from Taiwan. She collaborated with singer Kylie Minogue on a song called “In My Arms.”
