A Short Bio On Ja Morant

Ja Morant, aka G12, is an American professional basketball player currently playing for the Memphis Grizzlies in the NBA.

The point guard was born on August 10, 1999, in Dalzell, South Carolina, United States, to his father, Tee Morant, and mother, Jamie Morant.

Both his parents played basketball as high schooler; now we know where his skills and shooting ability comes from.

Growing up, Ja attended Crestwood High School in Sumter, South Carolina, and grew from 1.75 meters to 1.83 meters in his first three years with the Crestwood Knights Basketball team.

After high school graduation, Morant went to Murray State University and played college basketball for the Murray State Racers.

After the loss in the 2019 NCAA men’s basketball tournament, he announced his intention to declare for the NBA Draft, forgoing his final two seasons of colligate eligibility.

He had exceptional two years of college basketball and was drafted by the Grizzlies second overall during the 2019 NBA Draft.

He signed a four-year rookie contract with the team on July 2, 2019, and made his professional debut on October 23 in a 101-120 loss to the Miami Heat.

Similarly, Morant recorded his then career-high 22 points in a 131-119 loss to the San Antonio Spurs on December 23, 2020.

After the four-year deal came to an end, Ja signed a four-year extension with the Grizzlies on July 6, 2022.
