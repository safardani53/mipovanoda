MOLLY-MAE Hague fans have reacted furiously after she revealed her 'real size' in a candid social media post.

The Love Island star, who gave birth to daughter Bambi back in January, revealed she wears size 14 Zara clothes while restocking her designer wardrobe.

However, it isn't the influencer, 24, who is coming under fire, but rather the popular high street brand.

It has faced accusations on social media its items are being sized up as many can't believe Molly-Mae is a genuine 14.

One person wrote on Twitter: "Molly Mae wearing a size 14 in Zara shows how whack their sizing is because no way that girl is a 14."

Another posted: "I cannot shop in Zara every time I end up wanting to cry in the changing rooms."
