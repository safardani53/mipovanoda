The Kardashians have Christmas traditions going to this day that have been running for over four decades. When it comes to the Kar-Jenners’ Christmas Day schedule, they don’t mess around. Following the family members’ biggest night of the year – Christmas Eve – they opt for a slightly toned-down day on the 25.

During throwback episodes of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, fans will have seen the lengths the famous family goes to over the festive period. They’re hiring Santa impersonators, ice rinks, and transforming their homes into winter wonderlands. The Kardashians are often brought back down to earth by their kids, however, remember North West‘s dead hamster story over Christmas dinner?

Kardashian Christmas Day schedule: Fun begins on December 24

The Kardashian Christmas festivities kick off before Christmas Day. The Hulu stars have a tradition of celebrating each Christmas Eve with a huge party where they get dressed to the nines.

Then, of course, the big 25 rolls around and the family appears to opt for a slightly less glamorous affair on Christmas Day.

Last year, Kylie Jenner shared a sweet fresh-faced photo of herself and mom Kris Jenner wearing matching pajamas via Instagram.

Kim Kardashian also took to social media to share a snap surrounded by her kids, nephews, and nieces wearing matching red PJs.

The matching pajama theme goes back decades as Kris shared an old snap of herself, Kim, Kourtney Kardashian, and MJ on Instagram this Christmas Eve.

Khloé Kardashian didn’t seem to share any loungewear-clad photos to Instagram last year but she did post photos from a night she “looks forward to every year.”

The mom of two posed with her best friends Khadijah and Malika Haqq wearing a showstopping red gown.

Get together for Christmas morning

Christmas is Kris Jenner’s favorite time of the year and she’s passed on her enthusiasm for the festive period to her kids. She said she “looks forward to this time of year all year long.”

Every year, the family gets super competitive over their decorations. KUWTK saw Kim Kardashian, Kris, Kourtney, and co all scheming their way around Aldik’s, a Christmas shop in Los Angeles, California.

Once the decorating has been done, and the annual Christmas Eve party has been celebrated, the family often gets together again on Christmas morning.

Speaking on KUWTK, Kourtney said she was up until 3 am preparing Christmas food for her family after the Christmas Eve party ended at 12 am.

She said: “I was up until 3 am setting up the tables, the food, making the kitchen look perfect with everyone’s requests. It was a lot but I love it, just to have them all come in in the morning and have it all look so great. I love when it all comes together.”

Kris added the family made breakfast together, opened gifts, and “had a lot of fun together,” spending time with “everyone” on Christmas morning.

‘Battle of the Christmas mornings’

During KUWTK season 18, Kourtney explained hosting Christmas morning at her house for the whole family “means everything” to her.

Kylie threw a spanner in the works when she suggested having her own Christmas morning. The Kylie Cosmetics founder added it “doesn’t make sense” for her daughter “Santa only goes to Kourtney’s house.”

She suggested the idea of a “Christmas brunch,” but the rest of the siblings were keen to keep Kourtney’s Christmas morning tradition alive.

Kourtney said: “I’ve already agreed with the idea of everyone doing Christmas presents at their own houses but Christmas morning is mine and I want a really cozy, intimate moment with us and the kids.”

Judging by Kim’s recent Instagram Story, the family has all their gifts wrapped and ready to go in their own unique wrapping this year.

She shared she’s opting for SKIMS jersey material for her “reusable” gift wrap this year.
