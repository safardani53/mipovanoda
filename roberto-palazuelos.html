Who is Roberto Palazuelos:

Roberto Palazuelos is a famous TV Actor. He was born on January 31, 1967 and his birthplace is Acapulco, Mexico. Roberto is also well known as, Mexican actor who has appeared in films such as Hasta que la boda nos separe and Cofradía. He played the part of Santiago Sepúlveda in the TV series Entreolivos. 

Roberto is originated from Mexico. He acted alongside Marjorie de Sousa in the TV program Hasta el fin del mundo.
