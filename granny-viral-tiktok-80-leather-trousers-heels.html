My grannys 80 but looks decades younger - she even wears leather trousers and heels to the shops

Publish date: 2024-04-28

EVERYONE wants to age gracefully, but what about aging glamorously?

One grandmother is proving that age is just a number by wearing leather trousers and heels well into her eighties.

Ironically, this granny isn’t going viral on her own social media though.

The  80-year-old first became an internet sensation after appearing on her granddaughter’s, Isabella, Tiktok account.

Unlike the other influencers, Rigmore seems to avoid the spotlight and had previously only made a brief appearance as her loved one raided her wardrobe for vintage pieces.

In recent months though, the grandmother has developed an almost cult like following on Isabella’s page because of her apparent agelessness.

It's not just her style and her youthful skin that keep fans coming back though, with thousands gushing over her stunning Norwegian home and enviable designer wardrobe.

Even Isabella admits that her Tiktok has basically become a ‘fan page’ for her granny, with a recent video of her relative getting ready to get groceries gaining over 2.4 million views online.

It’s not hard to understand why, as the glam grandmother doesn’t nip out to get milk in joggers and an old t-shirt like everyone else.

Rather than sitting at home in the warm, the 80 year-old coordinates her outfits to her luxury handbag.

If that weren’t bougie enough, she even wears heels out to get bread – despite there being snow under foot.

Her first outfit though is more low key, with the glam granny arriving in black leggings and a glittering hoodie, which highlights her blonde hair.

Proving she not a ‘regular’ grandmother but a ‘cool’ grandmother, she adds the iconic Dior saddle bag and chunky brown fur trimmed boots that give major Mean Girls vibes.

Rigmore’s next look is a more sophisticated affair, with a beige hoodie and matching furry body warmer to keep her extra cosy during the weekly shop.

The cold weather won’t keep her from wearing heels though, as the 80-year-old matches her glittering silver stilettoes to her tiny handbag.

Her monochromatic looks keep coming with the glam granny later adding a fluffy purple jumper, matching heels and a large Chanel handbag in the same shade.

If she weren’t already looking luxurious enough, she then throws on some celeb-worthy sunnies and leather trousers.

Though she doesn’t have her own Tiktok account yet, it seems Isabella’s grandmother has amassed a huge following with people struggling to believe she is in her twilight years.

One fan even commented: “80?!?!!! No way”

Another added: “She's beautiful and doesn't look her age at all.”                            

“She is giving ‘go find a rich husband’ girl vibes,” joked a third, in awe of the multiple outfits.

Meanwhile, others compared her to Jennifer Collidge and Jessica Lange due to her glamourous aesthetic.
