Inside Witney Carson and Carson McAllister's Babymoon: Photos

Publish date: 2024-04-14

The calm before the storm. Pregnant Witney Carson and husband Carson McAllister enjoyed a babymoon in St. Lucia before she gives birth to their second child.

“We’ve been staying in an amazing villa with a pool and jacuzzi,” the Dancing With the Stars pro, 29, exclusively tells Us Weekly. “We’ve loved swinging on the bed swing at the beach and swimming in the ocean, then coming back to the room to lay out by our private pool.”

The couple just finished a relaxing trip to Sandals Grande St. Lucian Resort, and they stayed in the luxurious Rondoval Butler Suite.

The DWTS season 19 champ and the mechanical engineer, also 29, share 2-year-old son Kevin Leo. Since becoming parents, they’ve tried to prioritize getting some adults-only alone time.

“It’s always important to us to make a trip [for] just Carson and I once a year,” the Utah native explains to Us. “For the past two years, we’ve loved our experience at Sandals! We both think it’s crucial to make time for each other, especially when life gets crazy.”

The So You Think You Can Dance alum and her husband were high school sweethearts. They tied the knot in January 2016 and welcomed their son five years later. She announced during a November 2022 episode of Dancing With the Stars that baby No. 2 was on the way and revealed in January that she is expecting a little boy.

“We wanted this time to reconnect before entering this new chapter in our lives with two kids!” Carson gushed to Us of her babymoon at Sandals. “We are so excited to welcome this new baby boy!”

Previously, the two have enjoyed having active vacations at the Grande St. Lucian. “We love to take advantage of the all-inclusive scuba diving,” the choreographer reveals. “We got certified last year and it’s been one of our favorite things to do as a couple. We love the excursions and lounging by the gorgeous beach or pool!”

It’s been a more laid-back trip this time amid Carson’s pregnancy. “Last year we did lots of excursions and more activities, and this time around we relaxed by the beach and pool! Both trips were so amazing in different ways,” she tells Us.

The lovebirds enjoyed some golf at the Cap Estate Golf and Country Club and soaked up the sun by the water during their babymoon.
