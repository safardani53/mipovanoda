Publish date: 2024-05-13

DO YOU have a taste for the finer things in life, but can't afford the expensive price tags?

Don't worry, us too, but budget supermarket Aldi is providing the goods with a new range of luxury body products which are exactly like The White Company versions for a fraction of the price

You read that right - from today, you can stock up on Aldi's newest Paradise range with each product costing just £2.19.

Shoppers can save a whopping 85% compared to The White Company prices which range between £13-£15 for almost identical products.
