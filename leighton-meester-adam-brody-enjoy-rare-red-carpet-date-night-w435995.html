Leighton Meester, Adam Brody Enjoy Rare Red Carpet Date Night

Publish date: 2024-02-03

Well, hello, you two! Leighton Meester and Adam Brody stepped out for a rare red carpet date night at a screening for the O.C. alum’s new TV show, StartUp, on Tuesday, August 23, in Los Angeles.

The Gossip Girl alum, 30, supported her husband, 36, in a sleeveless, black lace top, teamed with black bottoms. She styled her honey-blonde hair into loose waves and modeled a fresh face with minimal makeup, including lightly blushed cheeks and a rose-hued lip.

Meanwhile, Brody looked dapper in a pastel plaid shirt, a slate blazer, slim-fit black pants and caramel suede shoes. While he posed solo on the red carpet, he found his wife inside the fete, where the two cozied up for photos.

Brody’s new dramatic series, which will premiere on Crackle this fall, is about a trio of people who launch a digital currency company (not unlike BitCoin) and the FBI agent who is determined to take them down. The series costars Martin Freeman, Edi Gathegi and Otmara Marrero.

It was a special occasion for the pair, as they haven’t been spotted on the red carpet together in more than two years! Their last official outing was at the Tony Awards in June 2014, where they celebrated Meester’s Broadway debut in Of Mice and Men. It was also their red carpet debut as a married couple.

Since then, the small-screen stars, who tied the knot in a secret ceremony in February 2014, have welcomed a daughter, Arlo Day, who just celebrated her first birthday on August 4.
