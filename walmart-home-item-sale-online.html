HOW TO SHOP WALMART

One of the best ways to score big markdowns is by shopping at Walmart's brick-and-mortar locations.

This is especially true for clearance items.

Oftentimes, you'll come across items in the store with yellow tags – indicating that there is a markdown.

However, there's a chance that a particular item on clearance can be marked down further.

To look into this yourself, you'll want to download the Walmart app and scan the barcode of the product – which will tell you the exact price.

A number of online influencers have done this including TikToker Chaz Dunn (@chazzzd).
