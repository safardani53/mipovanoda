Who is Vivica A Fox?

Vivica A Fox is an actress, producer, and TV host.

She was born on July 30, 1964, in South Bend, Indiana.

The 57-year-old actress has been in dozens of famous movies throughout her career.

Fox began her entertainment career as a dancer for Soul Train.

Some of Fox's hit movies include the following:

  • Independence Day
  • Set It Off
  • Two Can Play That Game
  • Booty Call
  • Soul Food
  • Kill Bill: Volume 1
  • Kill Bill 2
  • Getting Played

She appeared in the film Set It Off alongside Queen Latifah, Jada Pinkett Smith, and Kimberly Elise.
