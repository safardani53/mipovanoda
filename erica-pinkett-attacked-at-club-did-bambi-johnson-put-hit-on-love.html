Erica Pinkett Attacked at Club; Did Bambi Johnson Put Hit on Love &amp; Hip Hop Atlanta Star?

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta star Erica Pinkett was recently attacked in a nightclub, and says Bambi Johnson, one of her rivals on the show, is responsible for it.

The melee went down at Atlanta’s Taboo Club on Monday, June 9.

That night, a woman came up from behind Erica, hit her in the face, threw her to the ground and slashed her arm with a razor blade. No laughing matter.

Pinkett’s gash spewed blood, horror movie style, leading to more than just a crazy storyline for the VH1 show. A police investigation was opened.

The beating suspect left the club before cops arrived, but Stephanie Nicole Harp was arrested this morning for aggravated assault with a deadly weapon.

Erica believes someone else masterminded the hit, though.

Specifically, Bambi Johnson, with whom she has argued over Bambi’s boyfriend Lil Scrappy on more than one occasion, as fans of the show know.
