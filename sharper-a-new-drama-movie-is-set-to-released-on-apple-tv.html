Sharper A New Drama Movie Is Set To Released On Apple TV+

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Sharper, a new drama film from Apple TV+, will launch on the streaming platform on Friday, February 17, 2023. The plot revolves on a young lady seeking vengeance against his family while duping other rich individuals. Julianne Moore has a big part in the film, while numerous others play crucial supporting ones. The film is directed by Benjamin Caron, who also co-wrote the script with Alessandro Tanaka. On February 10, 2023, the film was released in select cinemas.

Sharper on Apple TV+ will send shivers down your spine.

On January 12, 2023, Apple TV released the official trailer for Sharper, providing a glimpse into the film’s myriad enigmatic and dramatic events. The trailer begins with a voiceover that outlines the film’s themes:

“Here’s some advice: if you’re going to steal, steal a lot.”

The trailer is about two and a half minutes long, yet it contains no big spoilers that may damage the experience of viewers. In the classic A24 manner, it retains a strange and somewhat bizarre tone. Along with the teaser, Apple TV posted the official summary of the thriller film on their YouTube account, stating:

“In Sharper, a neo-noir thriller of secrets and lies set in New York City’s bedrooms, barrooms, and boardrooms, no one is who they appear. Characters battle for wealth and power in a high-stakes game of ambition, greed, passion, and envy that will keep spectators guessing until the end.”

According to the teaser and description, audiences can expect a sophisticated and perplexing psychological thriller that delves into the darkest and most terrifying aspects of the human psyche. Following its limited theatrical debut earlier this month, the film earned largely mixed-to-positive reviews from reviewers, with many applauding the cast’s performances and visual flair, among other things.

More information about the Sharper cast and crew

Sharper showcases legendary actress Julianne Moore as Madeline in the primary role. Madeline is an ambitious and charming lady seeking to advance in the business world. According to multiple sources and the trailer, Madeline’s character serves as the film’s emotional center, and her journey defines the film’s thematic components. Julianne Moore looks fantastic in the clip, capturing her character’s peculiarities, ruthlessness, and ferocious attitude. In the film, viewers may anticipate her to provide a tremendous performance.

Apart from Sharper, Julianne Moore has played memorable roles in films such as Boogie Nights, Far From Heaven, Safe, and many more. Sebastian Stan, who plays Max with Moore, plays another important part. Based on the teaser, Max seems to be a well-known con artist. There isn’t much further information about his character at this moment, but fans should anticipate him to play an important part in the plot.

Other notable performances by Sebastian Stan include The Falcon and the Winter Soldier, Pam & Tommy, and Gossip Girl, to mention a few. Scott and Bailey, The Crown, Wallander, and more films have been directed by Benjamin Caron.
