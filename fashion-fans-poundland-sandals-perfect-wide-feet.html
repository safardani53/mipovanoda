Fashion fans love Poundlands sandals that are perfect for wide feet - and theyre only 6

Publish date: 2024-05-10

FINDING a decent pair of summer sandals can be tough if you have wide feet.

But fashion fans claim to have found the perfect ones - and they cost just £6 from Poundland.

"If you're like me and have wide feet Poundland/ pep&co do these for £6," one woman wrote on the Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group on Facebook.

"I find the sides dig in on the edges of my feet normally but don’t get that with these they are so soft and comfy.

"The straps are moveable and the leather material is so soft too and doesn’t rub or any thing."

In fact, she loved the shoes "so much" that she went back to get another pair in cream.
