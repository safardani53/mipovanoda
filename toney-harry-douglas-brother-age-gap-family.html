Who Is Toney Douglas, Harry Douglas Brother? Age Gap &amp; Family

Publish date: 2024-08-12

While Harry Douglas keeps trending due to his contributions across the ESPN network, many are intrigued to know his brother, Toney.

They became the sixth brothers to play in the NFL and NBA, respectively. It is a testament to their shared passion for sports.

Harry Douglas IV is not just a name associated with football. It’s a brand of athletic prowess and media leadership.

His journey began at Jonesboro High School, where he showcased his versatility by excelling in basketball, baseball, football, and track.

This early dedication and talent earned him an athletic scholarship to the University of Louisville. He continued to shine as a standout athlete throughout his collegiate career.

After graduating in 2007 with a BA in Political Science, Harry’s professional career took off when he was drafted in 2008 as the 83rd pick player by the Atlanta Falcons.

Over the next ten years, he played a pivotal role in the success of the Atlanta Falcons and later with the Tennessee Titans.

Currently, he contributes to ESPN as an analyst, co-hosting ESPN College Game Day and co-hosting the Atlanta Falcons pregame/post-game.

Harry Douglas Brother, Toney Douglas

In the world of basketball, another Douglas has made a name for himself – Toney Bernard Douglas.

Toney began his college basketball career at Auburn before transferring to Florida State University.

The Los Angeles Lakers drafted him as the 29th overall pick in the 2009 NBA Draft.

Regardless, Toney’s professional journey led him through various NBA teams. The list includes the Rockets, Warriors, Kings, Miami Heat, Pelicans, and Grizzlies.

Toney also took his talents overseas, playing for The Jiangsu Dragons in China. He played with prominent teams in Turkey, Italy, Spain, Greece, and Israel.

Currently, he continues his professional basketball career with Benfica in the Liga Portuguesa de Basquetebol.

Douglas Brother Age Gap

Harry and Toney Douglas share more than just a last name; they share a strong bond as best friends.

The Douglas brothers were born 18 months apart, Harry on September 16, 1984, and Toney on March 16, 1986.

While their birthplace was Tampa, Florida, the Douglas brothers grew up in Jonesboro, Georgia.

Although Douglas often got the best of his little brother, their brotherly bickering was largely limited to one-on-one games.

They were inseparable from the beginning, playing on the same teams and sharing interests.

The Douglas brothers’ journey started early in Jonesboro, where they played travel baseball and various other sports together.

Their camaraderie extended to the high school basketball team, drawing crowds so large that the fire department had to turn fans away.

Through thick and thin, the Douglas brothers stood by each other, making memories and supporting one another.

The Douglas Family

The foundation of Harry and Toney’s upbringing consists of parents, Harry and Stephanie Douglas, and older sister Jamila.

Raised in Jonesboro, Georgia, the Douglas siblings share a tight-knit family bond.

Harry has been married to his lovely wife, Kierra Douglas, since June 2013. Moreover, he is a proud father of two children, Harrison and Harry V.

Toney, on the other hand, was previously married to his college sweetheart, Sherrice Bryson Douglas.

He welcomed their son Toney Jr. with Sherrice in May 2012. Unfortunately, Toney’s marriage ended in divorce, and details about his current relationship remain private.
