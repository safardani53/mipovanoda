Publish date: 2024-07-21

LEARN how to style your bra and make it invisible under your white shirt with these tips from an expert.

Nicola Crook, a trained bra fitting expert, dished out the best bra colors to wear under a white or semi-sheer shirt depending on your skin tone.

Nicola is the founder and CEO of Bras and Honey, so she knows the best secrets that you need for your closet.

In her YouTube video, Nicola tried on a white, nude, black, and even burgundy and red bra under her white, semi-sheer top.

She had promised that the results would shock you and she didn't disappoint.

The number one rule is to not wear a white bra with a white top. The bra will show through the top, no matter your skin tone.

If you have a lighter skin tone, Nicola suggests wearing a nude bra under your white shirt.

A black bra works best if you have a darker skin tone, according to the expert.

Surprisingly, you can also wear a burgundy bra under a white top if you have a darker skin tone.

The most surprising discovery that Nicola made was with the red bra. You would think that a bright color like red would definitely show through a white or semi-sheer top, but no.

The color red has been "one of the fashion industry's best-kept secrets" because it's invisible under white, Nicola said.

It has to do with the pigmentation of your skin. Many people's skin, no matter their tone, has a pinkish or red undertone.

This means a red bra would match your skin, making it invisible under your white or semi-sheer top.

Nicola isn't the only expert to help you get the perfect look with your bras.

TikToker @Americanthreads has the best hack for making your boobs look bigger and perkier without a push-up bra.

In a video, she passes a piece of string through both of her bra straps across the front of her chest.

She then pulls the string through to the center of her chest, tying a knot.
