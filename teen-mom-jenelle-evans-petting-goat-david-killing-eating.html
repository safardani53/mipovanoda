Teen Moms Jenelle Evans shares video petting family goat after David bragged about KILLING and EA

Publish date: 2024-10-07

JENELLE Evans shared videos of herself petting and sweet-talking her pet goats just hours after husband David Eason boasted about eating one of their other goats.

Fans were left outraged after the Teen Mom alum’s husband talked about killing the goat and showed off the animal's severed head.

In videos shared to her Instagram Story on Monday, Jenelle walked up to where all of her goats were gated in and started to pet one of them.

She stuck out her hand to stroke the goat’s back and used her sweet voice while talking to the animal as it got closer to her, as well as the wire fence: “To my baby. Don’t get stuck. Don’t you get stuck.”

The mother of three then turned her attention onto another one of her goats, saying: “Come here, Joseph. Let me pet you. 

“Come here. Can I pet you? Don’t bite me.”

The reality star’s videos of being kind and tender with the animals come just shortly after her husband bragged about killing and eating the family's pet goal Elvis.

The animal had been born on their farm and raised by Jenelle and David for over a year.

David, who admitted to killing the family dog, Nugget, in 2019, once again shocked fans with a new video posted online about it all.

On Saturday, he showed off "goat nuggets" as he cooked them in the family kitchen before giving further detail on Sunday.

The former Teen Mom 2 star first showed off two pieces of raw meat where he wrote: "This is where goat nuggets come from, for all those asking."

He addressed the camera directly as he bit into a grilled piece of meat: "If you're wondering what a goat taste like off the grill, well I'm here to tell you: tastes like chicken, tastes a little like beef or pork and it's real good."

David then dropped the bomb when he told his fans that "this is Elvis, the little baby we had last year."

"It's f***ing delicious, people should eat more goat."

He further addressed the non-believers and showed off the goat's severed head in the trash and said: "For those who don't believe it was goat, it is. His name was Elvis."

Not only were fans shocked because David showed off the severed head on social media, but because this goat had been his and Jenelle's kids' pet.

One fan wrote that David "has zero respect" and another said neither of them have "integrity."

This incident comes just one year after David admitted to killing their dog, Nugget, and justified it by saying she "was a loving dog to me but she was not loving towards our kids at all."
