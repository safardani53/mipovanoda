I'm a cleaning whizz and my mopping hack is a total game changer - my whole house smells amazing and

Publish date: 2024-08-05

NO ONE enjoys mopping the floor, but unfortunately it's just one of those things that needs to be done.

But one cleaning whizz shared how she blitzes her floors in no time, leaving the entire house smelling amazing and there's never any residue left either.

The mum shared how she mops her floors like a pro on YouTube and viewers loved her tips and tricks.

Despite trying many different products and methods for cleaning her hardwood floors, the keen cleaner has found the perfect solution.

"I've already swept the floor, and then I went over it with the vacuum cleaner to get anything the broom might've left behind," she said.

Although the floors already looked clean the mum said "I'll show you the mopping hack and the dirty water that comes out, you will not believe your eyes."

The cleaning whizz said she uses a spin mop for the best results, along with hot water.

"You don't want to boil it, but it needs to be right before it gets to a boil," she explained.

The mum added a laundry detergent pod and a splash of any liquid cleaner you like the smell of.

"The reason this works so great is because the laundry detergent is formulated to be a degreaser, to strip residue and things like that."

The liquid cleaner is added mostly for the scent which is spread throughout the entire house thanks the the hot steam.

"It makes a big difference," she quipped.

Not only that but the method "dries a whole lost faster than a sponge mop," the mum raved.

"It does a good job and saves us money."
