How long has Andy Cohen hosted Watch What Happens Live?

Publish date: 2024-05-01

ANDY Cohen has been America's tour guide through all things pop-culture since the early 2000s.

He has been such a staple of pop-culture and celebrity commentary that he has become a celebrity himself.

How long has Andy Cohen hosted Watch What Happens Live?

Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen has been on since June 16, 2009.

The first episode welcomed Danielle Staub, Sarah Jessica Parker, and Reco Chapple.

On the late-night talk show, Andy discusses pop culture topics with his celebrity and reality TV star guest every week.

The show airs on Bravo and centers on Bravo related guest and topics.

The live format of the show allows for interaction with viewers by phone and social media.

What What Happens Live with Andy Cohen is on its 18th season has been on the air for 12 years with over 1,500 episodes.

What else does Andy Cohen do?

Andy is a radio and television personality, producer, and writer.

Besides hosting Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, Andy has a pop-culture channel on SirisXM called Radio Andy.

Andy worked as Bravo's executive vice president of Development and Talent until 2013 and still serves as executive producer of the Real Housewives Franchise.

His autobiographical memoir Most Talkative released in May 2012 became a New York Times Bestseller and his second memoir The Andy Cohen Diaries: A Deep Look at a Shallow Year released in November 2014 also became a Times Bestseller.

Andy is constantly gracing our screens whether it be hosting the Miss America pageant or playing himself in a guest spot on Riverdale.

Hoping to ring in a new and better year, Andy co-hosted CNN's 2020 New Year's Eve show with his friend Anderson Cooper.
