Who is Ted Koppy? | The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-06-03

TED Koppy has become a trusted face throughout Connecticut as the state's news anchor for NBC.

Here's everything to know about the life and career of NBC's Ted Koppy.

Who is Ted Koppy?

Ted Koppy has spent the last two decades building his name as a journalist.

After graduating from Saint John's University, Koppy went on to become an anchor for KDLT-TV in South Dakota.

In 1996, Koppy moved back to New York to become the late-night weekday anchor for WHEC-TV 10 NBC.

He spent the next 14 years working as an anchor for multiple networks before temporarily turning in his hat as a journalist and deciding to work in the financial industry.

From 2011-2016, Koppy worked as a financial advisor for Waddel & Reed.

While working as a financial advisor, Koppy went back into journalism in 2014 working as an anchor for News 12 Connecticut before officially becoming NBC Connecticut's news anchor in November of 2016.

Koppy can be seen on weekday mornings from 4.30-7am on NBC Connecticut Today.

Is Ted Koppy married and does he have kids?

Ted Koppy currently lives in Milford, CT with his wife, Maria, and their two daughters.

Maria and Ted have been married for over 20 years.

Though Ted is very private about his personal life, Ted has often paid tribute to Maria.

He once posted a picture of his wife and two daughters as a promotion for NBC CT's superhero moms segment captioned: "Maria, BTW, has been a super-duper Superhero to our family for more than 20 years!"

What is his net worth?

According to multiple sites, Ted Koppy has an estimated net worth ranging between $1million to $5million.

Throughout his broadcasting career, Koppy has won two Emmy awards.

He has also won an Edward R. Murrow award.
