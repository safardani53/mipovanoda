Travis Barker Unveils His Spooky Christmas Tree Display

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Travis Barker is drawing inspiration from his Halloween decorations this holiday season! 

In a photo shared to his Instagram Story on Monday, the Blink-182 drummer, 48, revealed the spooky twist he added to his Christmas tree display this year. 

A giant skeleton — that looks similar to Home Depot’s famous 12-foot statue — appears to be lending a helping hand with decorating this season as it holds a string of white lights that are connected to the tree. 

The massive figure towers next to the glittering evergreen adorned with a simple layer of bulbs. In front of the skeleton, a silver stereo can be seen facing the tree, while a red-and-white striped drum set sits in the background. 

Barker’s wife, Kourtney Kardashian Barker, also got into the holiday spirit on Monday when she revealed her elaborate Elf on the Shelf display and lit-up Christmas tree on her Instagram Story. 

As theme music from the movie Elf played in the background of the post, the Lemme founder, 44, shared a peek at her homemade festive display featuring four sledding Elf on the Shelf dolls. 

“Elf Sledding Now Open,” was written at the top of the display above the elves that were stuck to silver foil plates meant to represent their sleds. Meanwhile, the cotton balls that surrounded them were used to depict snow.  

“Elf season has begun,” she captioned the photo. 

Barker and Kardashian Barker welcomed their first baby together, son Rocky Thirteen, earlier in November. The pair revealed they were expecting in June, just one month after they celebrated the one-year anniversary of their Santa Barbara courthouse wedding. 

The Kardashians star is also a mom to daughter Penelope, 11, and sons Reign, 8, and Mason, 13, with her ex Scott Disick. Barker is also a dad to daughter Alabama, 17, and son Landon, 19, plus stepdaughter Atiana De La Hoya, 24, with his ex-wife Shanna Moakler.

Following the birth of Rocky, a source exclusively told PEOPLE that Kardashian Barker was “over the moon” about her son’s arrival. "Her pregnancy took a stressful turn towards the end," they added. “She is happy to just be able to snuggle her baby boy now. She feels so blessed."

Shortly after the mom of four welcomed her newborn into the world, she held a lavish vegan Thanksgiving dinner to celebrate her husband’s 48th birthday.
