ASAP Rocky Is Charged With Felony Firearm Assault For A 2021 Shooting

Publish date: 2024-08-21

ASAP Rocky has been charged with criminal assault with a handgun in connection with his alleged role in a shooting incident in 2021. According to L.A. County District Attorney George Gascon, firing a pistol in a public area is a crime that may have resulted in disastrous repercussions for the target and other persons there. In addition, Gascon stated:

“After thoroughly reviewing the evidence in this case, my office concluded that the inclusion of a particular firearm charge was merited.”

Rocky’s arraignment is set on August 17 at Foltz Criminal Justice Center Department 30. The shooting investigation is still ongoing, according to the LAPD.
