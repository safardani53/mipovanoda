Meet Too Hot To Handle season 2 star

Joey Joy entered the Turks and Caicos set of Netflix’s Too Hot To Handle as a late comer in the second season.

Rescuing Carly after breaking up with Chase, Joey is well-liked and shared a little about his life, such as being from Miami and his aim for the show.

The star claimed that his “game plan” was to “be me, stay me.”

Joey’s Instagram bio displays three flags representing the United States, Greece and Italy.

Although it does not seem like he has put his degree to use as of yet, according to his Westminster athlete profile, Joey majored in Business Administration.

As a senior in 2019, Joey practically graduated and then joined the Too Hot To Handle cast when it was taped in late 2020.
