Roger William Allam is an English actor, best known for his theatrical career, even though he has acted in film, television and radio. He played Inspector Javert in the London original work of the musical Les Misérables, First Officer Douglas Richardson in the award-winning radio series Cabin Pressure and DI Fred Thursday in the TV series Endeavor. He is also known for his roles as Illyrio Mopatis in the HBO Game of Thrones series, Royalton in Speed ​​Racer, Lewis Prothero in the 2005 adaptation of V for Vendetta, and as congressman Peter Mannion in The Thick Of It. He has been four times nominated for the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor, twice winning.
