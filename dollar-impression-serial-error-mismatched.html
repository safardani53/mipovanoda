Your $1 bill could be worth $16,800 - the 'impression' detail to look for

Publish date: 2024-02-26

A DOLLAR bill from 1995 has sold for nearly $17,000 thanks to a few key serial number errors.

Despite seeming at first glance to be an ordinary $1 bill, the note has two very distinct errors setting it apart.

Shaun, who runs the rare coin and dollar TikTok channel BlueRidgeSilverHound, profiled the bill, with an "impression" detail to look for, in a new video for his over 14,000 followers.

First, it has mismatched serial numbers.

And while bills with even one mismatched serial number can command big sales at an auction, all eight numbers on this bill are mismatched, adding to its rareness.

On top of that major error, this bill has another odd mistake.

Right next to the serial number is a colon-like impression of the ink wheel used to print the number.

The bill has a paper money grading (PMG) rating of 25 out of 70, which is toward the lower end of the grading scale.

Despite its mediocre quality rating, the bill sold for $16,800 at a Stack's Bowers auction.

SERIAL ERRORS

Notes with mismatched serial numbers are highly prized by collectors.

Most read in Money

And some of the more valuable errors are the smallest and hardest to spot.

A $20 dollar bill with mismatched suffixes on the two serial numbers sold for nearly $2,000.

One serial number ended with a D, the other with an F.

And a $1 bill with a star next to one serial number but not another sold for nearly $30,000.

OTHER NOTES TO LOOK FOR

Old, discontinued bills in good condition rival serial number bills as the most sought-after.

A colorful "rainbow" $5 bill from 1869 with Andrew Jackson on the front sold for $5,300.

And a 1928, $1,000 bill with Grover Cleveland on the front sold for over five times its already large face value - $5,412.

And a 1934, $500 bill featuring William McKinley sold for $7,245.

In 1969, $500, $1,000, $5,000, and $10,000 bills were discontinued, making each surviving example in good condition worth even more than its hefty denomination.
