The Best Met Gala 2023 Red Carpet Looks

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Anne Hathaway

Anne Hathaway brings the heat in a tweed gown held together with pearl-embellished safety pins, all custom Atelier Versace.

Cardi B

Cardi B pays tribute to Karl in a ballgown with rosette-embellished skirt, a yoke inspired by his signature look and a platinum wig.

Margot Robbie

Margot Robbie continues in her Chanel ambassador role in a black silk chiffon dress embellished with clear plastic and gold metal chains from the Haute Couture Spring-Summer 1993 collection, with accessories from the brand.

Priyanka Chopra & Nick Jonas

Cute couple Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas, who first connected at the Met Gala, have a date night in matching black-and-white by Valentino: a leather blazer and skinny tie for him, and a black bow-embellished gown with opera gloves and coat for her, plus a Bulgari necklace set with an 11-carat blue diamond estimated to fetch $25 million when it goes on sale at Sotheby's later this month.

Gigi Hadid

Gigi Hadid wears a bustier, briefs and Calzedonia tights under patterned tulle, draped with patterned tulle, plus Lagos pearl jewelry.

Olivia Rodrigo

Singer Olivia Rodrigo's gown pays graphic tribute to some Chanel signatures, including rosettes and a black-and-white palette, by Thom Browne, plus Lorraine Schwartz jewelry.

Idris Elba & Sabrina Elba

Idris Elba and Sabrina Elba went for the classics in coordinated looks by Gucci.

Viola Davis

Viola Davis picks an exuberant fuchsia Valentino gown with feathers and petals and adds Boucheron jewelry.

Stephanie Hsu

Stephanie Hsu pairs her Lagerfeld tribute collar with a gradient pailette-adorned column gown by Valentino and graphic eye makeup.

Karlie Kloss

Supermodel Karlie Kloss (and her "plus one" — a baby on the way!) wears bespoke Loewe with printed pearl and chain details, plus Garrard jewelry, Jennifer Behr bow and Jimmy Choo mules.

Amanda Seyfried

Amanda Seyfried puts on a show(girl) in her Oscar de la Rents mini created from chains of sparkling beads, plus champagne Stuart Weitzman heels, Cartier jewelry and red lips.

Pedro Pascal

Pedro Pascal wears shorts, patent boots and a crimson overcoat and dress shirt, all Valentino.

Salma Hayek

Salma Hayek wears a scarlet Gucci gown with latex bustier, tiers of tulle ruffles over lace tights, a bow at the waist and pearls at her arms (plus more at her earlobes by Lorraine Schwartz).

Doja Cat

Putting the Cat in Doja Cat, the singer gives a Choupette homage in a formfitting silver gown with tulle-accented train and built-in ears on the hood.

Simu Liu

Wearing custom Atelier Versace consisting of a pleated tux, leather gloves and cravat (with jewelry by T+C Goutal, Jacob & Co. and Vacheron Constantin), Simu Liu is serving looks.

Jenna Ortega

Jenna Ortega strikes a pose in a tweed Thom Browne ballgown with deconstructed skirt, woven through with chains and pearls and finished with a bow tie and spectator platforms.

Mindy Kaling

Mindy Kaling wears a silvery Simkhai ballgown with a structured bodice and train embellished with floral details, worn over a shimmering column skirt with Fred Leighton and Kwiat jewels.

Madelyn Cline

Glass Onion star Madelyn Cline wears a black strapless Stella McCartney gown embellished with silver fringe, plus Boucheron diamonds.

Jessica Chastain

Jessica Chastain let the night's theme go to her head (get it?) with her one-night-only platinum coif and shades, an homage to Karl Lagerfeld's signature look. She picked a tiered tulle Gucci ballgown and sheer opera gloves, plus statement jewelry.

Yara Shahidi

Yara Shahidi wears a micromini with a mother-of-pearl accented bustier and flowing train detailing, plus some mega jewels by Cartier and Jimmy Choo heels.

Michaela Coel

Michaela Coel, one of the night's co-chairs, wore a bronze-and-gold gown hand-embroidered with a celestial motif by Schiaparelli Haute Couture.

Quinta Brunson

Abbot Elementary star Quinta Brunson goes ultra-glam in a camellia-accented pale pink gown with black mesh overlay and matching black gloves, plus an elaborate updo and De Beers jewelry.

Vanessa Hudgens

Vanessa Hudgens wears a Michael Kors Collection black-and-white gown with rosettes at the bodice and criss-cross halter, plus Roberto Coin jewelry.

Bradley Cooper

Bradley Cooper wears a black Tom Ford tux and aviators.

Anitta

Singer Anitta pairs her elegant black ruched Marc Jacobs gown and white gloves with some monster platform Mary Janes and Tiffany & Co. jewelry.

Hugh Jackman & Deborra-Lee Furness

Hugh Jackman & Deborra-Lee Furness bring their best black-and-white glam to the carpet in a classic Zegna tux for him and crackle-effect shimmery gown by Tom Ford for her with Lorraine Schwartz jewels.

Lea Michele

Lea Michele promised a Barbra Streisand tribute on her Instagram, and did so with a silver open-back Michael Kors Collection dress with bow details and pageboy bob, plus jewelry by Fred Leighton, Kwiat and Jacob & Co.

Jeremy Strong

Jeremy Strong wears a custom Prada evening coat over a jade ruffled tuxedo shirt with yellow floral pendant.

Christina Ricci

Christina Ricci wears a two-piece Fendi Couture gown in a shimmering champagne fabric with Gismondi 1754 jewelry.

Phoebe Bridgers

Singer Phoebe Bridgers wears a black Tory Burch gown with pleated train and delicate pearl embroidery including draped off-the-shoulder sleeves.

Naomi Campbell

Naomi Campbell is giving goddess in a draped pink satin gown over silver sequin and glass beadwork from the Haute Couture Spring-Summer 2010 collection, plus Chanel High Jewelry.

Jack Harlow

Jack Harlow wears a navy tweed suit with burgundy shirt and sparkly brooch.

Maluma

Maluma wears a sleeveless gray Boss suit with white shawl and sparkling jewels.

Mary J. Blige

Mary J. Blige wears a plunging, high-slit Burberry gown embroidered with cobalt roses, plus matching boots and a Tyler Ellis clutch and Lorraine Schwartz jewelry.

Miranda Kerr

Miranda Kerr wears a shimmering Dior Haute Couture gown with jewel-embroidered straps, plus jewelry by Dior.

Allison Williams

Allison Williams dons peach satin with a large origami bow at the bodice — a custom Patou creation that paid homage to one Lagerfeld designed for the house in 1959. She added black gloves and Mikimoto pearl jewels.

Suki Waterhouse & Robert Pattinson

Suki Waterhouse wears a vintage Fendi gown from 2019 designed by Karl Lagerfeld with Reza jewelry, while boyfriend Robert Pattinson selects a Dior Mens by Kim Jones navy suit, for their first Met Gala as a couple.

Halle Bailey

Halle Bailey gets her Disney Princess on in an iridescent floral halter gown by Gucci with shimmering tulle-tiered wrap and De Beers jewelry.

Bad Bunny

Bad Bunny wears a white single-button suit with rosette-adorned wrap by Jacquemus, with an open back and subtle tributes to Lagerfeld including a photo printed inside the jacket.

Ke Huy Quan

Ke Huy Quan wears a Dior Mens pinstripe suit with fingerless leather gloves, jewels by Fred Leighton and Kwiat and Omega watch.

Pierce Brosnan & Keely Shaye Brosnan

Pierce Brosnan and Keely Shaye Brosnan keep it classic in an embossed black tux for him and a high-shine gown with sparkling sheer cape for her.

Alexandra Daddario

Alexandra Daddario wore Dior Haute Couture with layers of beaded ruffles and a sheer cape with Boucheron jewelry.

Josh Groban & Natalie McQueen

Singer Josh Groban makes his red carpet couples debut with new love Natalie McQueen, both wearing classic black-and-white evening attire (including his Stephen Dweck brooch).

Patrick Mahomes & Brittany Mahomes

Patrick Mahomes (in Christian Louboutin shoes) and Brittany Mahomes wear classic black and white looks by Boss with a little sparkling detail.

Kim Petras

Kim Petras pairs glittering platform boots with her black-and-white Marc Jacobs mini.

Bella Ramsey

Bella Ramsey accented their jewel-embellished three-piece suit look by Thom Browne with jewels by Eva Fehren and Sydney Evan.

Maude Apatow

Maude Apatow wears a mod black cutout column gown with cheeky silver detailing by Chloé, inspired by a vintage design of Lagerfeld's for the house, plus Cartier jewelry.

Lily James

Lily James paired her leather bustier ballgown with sheer gloves and choppy bangs, plus De Beers jewelry.

Lily Aldridge

Lily Aldridge takes a bow in a black gown with bow-adorned sleeves and a large pink bow at the back.

Teyana Taylor

Teyana Taylor mixes business with pleasure in a suit-inspired tweed gown by Thom Browne with bold hip cutouts, a veiled hat and Grace Lee jewelry.

Jodie Comer

Jodie Comer wears a violet, black and white one-shoulder gown by Burberry with sparkling Fred Leighton jewels.

Ice Spice

Ice Spice wears a high-neck white gown with sheer panels, with Effy Jewelry and a camera-shaped clutch.

Gwendoline Christie

Wearing Fendi Couture by Kim Jones, Gwendoline Christie goes for head-to-toe blush pink.

Devon Aoki

Devon Aoki wears a Jeremy Scott gown with wing detail at the bodice and a sequin skirt, plus Gismondi 1754 jewelry.

Camila Morrone

Camila Morrone wears a gown with white lace yoke and black fitted and flared skirt, plus Fred Leighton jewels and a Jennifer Behr bow.

Ariana DeBose

Ariana DeBose does the thing in a yellow, tracksuit-inspired two piece gown with faux mink trim by Altuzarra, plus dangling earrings and tons of rings.

Usher

Usher attends in a Bianca Saunders look accessorized with Lorraine Schwartz jewels, gloves and Christian Louboutin loafers.

Letitia Wright

Letitia Wright suits up in a black double-breasted Prada tux with sparkling wide-lapel shirt and Melissa Kaye jewelry.

Erykah Badu

Erykah Badu wore white fringe by Marni and layers of necklaces.

Ben Platt

Ben Platt feels the need for tweed in a cropped, piped jacket with matching wide-leg pants and a chain belt, plus David Yurman jewelry.

Glenn Close

Glenn Close renders the night's big silhouette (with collars, cuffs and necklaces, plus the oversized wrap) in a refreshing mint green.

Trevor Noah

Trevor Noah wears a velvet embroidered jacket by Thom Browne and patent shoes, plus De Beers jewelry.

Sienna Miller

Sienna Miller wears a fully feathered Gucci gown with silver Christian Louboutin heels and long pearl drop earrings.

Rita Ora

Rita Ora accents her peekaboo gown with exposed boning and long train with a manicure consisting of yards of chains and pearls (an homage to some signature Chanel design elements) and Jimmy Choo heels.

Taika Waititi

Director (and Rita Ora's husband) Taika Waititi wears a double-breasted gunmetal coat with chains of pearls and a floral brooch, plus Cartier jewelry and Manolo Blahnik shoes.
