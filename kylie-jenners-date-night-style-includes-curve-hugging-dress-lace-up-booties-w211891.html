Kylie Jenners Date Night Style Includes Curve-Hugging Dress, Lace-Up Booties

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Sultry date-night style! Kylie Jenner dressed up in a body-hugging midi-length frock for dinner with on-again boyfriend Tyga in West Hollywood on Thursday, June 30.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 18, showed off her hourglass figure in the clingy nude dress, which she paired with a black duster coat, a matching handbag and sexy, lace-up booties.

Jenner let the outfit speak for itself, opting for a minimal makeup look that left her with fresh-faced vibes. To complete the getup, she styled her long dark hair parted to the side and straight, falling down around her ribcage.

Tyga was dressed more casually for the occasion, rocking dark gym shorts with white stripe details near the hem, a white tee and red sneakers. He topped off his ensemble with a navy and red baseball hat and added a little sparkle with stud earrings.

As Us Weekly previously reported, the Kylie Lip Kit creator and her 26-year-old rapper beau have rekindled their romance over the past week, after a high-profile split in May.

A source confirmed to Us that Tyga, who shares son King Cairo with Rob Kardashian‘s fiancée, Blac Chyna, moved in with Jenner on Tuesday, June 28. “They are officially back together and he is staying there with her,” the source said. “It’s up in the air if he is going to live there permanently or is just staying there for now.”
