How Did Matt Iseman's Gay Rumors Originate? Is the Comedian Married Now?

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Fans began to question Clean House star Matt Iseman's gender identity after the fashion designer and Queer Eye star Carson Kressley posted a convincing picture with Iseman on Twitter back in 2017.

Since the post came into existence, fans have been arduously querying about Iseman's relationship status and orientation.

Who Is Matt Iseman?

Iseman is a comedian, actor, and television host who began his professional journey as a doctor. He is renowned for his role as play-by-play announcer of American Ninja Warrior.

In early 2017, he became the first winner of The New Celebrity Apprentice.

His Gay Rumors

The television host has not made any official statement to settle down the rumor mill set ablaze by Kressley's controversial tweet. The tweet in question features Iseman carrying the Queer Eye star in his arm, who, in return, seems pretty excited by the act. 

Almost as if pouring hot gasoline to the already fired-up rumor mill, the snap was accompanied by a caption that read, "@mattiseman and I leave on a gay cruise in the am! #congratulations." 

Matters got even more suspicious when Iseman appeared on RuPaul's Celebrity Drag Race and participated in one of the fantastic lip sync battles. If that statement did not make much sense to you, check out the Instagram post that Iseman put up on occasion below.

Iseman's Dating History

The speculations made about his orientation based on a few social media posts can't hold their water when put against the fact that Iseman had a girlfriend.

Although his current relationship status is shrouded in mystery, he was reportedly in a relationship once as his then-girlfriend even made an appearance on a video on his YouTube channel.

In the video uploaded on November 9, 2016, the comedian revealed her first name as Joe. Iseman also made a humorous remark on the description section, where he stated that he was clearly an expert when it came to making things work with a partner under the same roof.

I give the scoop on how to make it work with your special someone under the same roof. Because I am clearly an expert.

That's not all! There are records of past tweets where the American Ninja Warrior host acknowledged having a girlfriend. Sadly, the tweets have since been deleted.

His Views on Getting Married and Wife

In 2013, the Emmy Award-winning show host announced, in a now-deleted tweet, that he was going home to celebrate his parents' 50th wedding anniversary. He further added that he'd better start taking vitamins and get married if he wanted a shot at the same milestone.

But even nearly 8 years since that tweet, there is no news about his marriage yet.

In another instance, in yet another deleted tweet from 2014, Iseman shared his thoughts on divorces in Hollywood. He claimed that he was doing his part in keeping Hollywood divorces at a low by not getting married in the first place.

All in all, it can be stated that Iseman is interested in the ladies. However, since Iseman is known to keep his personal life under wraps, it isn't easy to put the finger on his current relationship status or orientation.
