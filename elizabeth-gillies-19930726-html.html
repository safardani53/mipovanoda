Elizabeth Egan Gillies is an American actress and singer. Gillies made her Broadway debut at the age of 15 in the musical 13, playing the character of Lucy. She first appeared on television in The Black Donnellys, then played Jade West in Nickelodeon's Victorious series. From 2011 to 2014, she voiced Daphne in Nickelodeon's Winx Club, and she starred in the comedy series FX Sex & Drugs & Rock & Roll. Since 2017, Gillies has played the billionaire heir and energy director Fallon Carrington in the series The CW's Dynasty, a reboot of the 1980s series of the same name. Various TV series, including the horror film Animal, the TV series Killing Daddy, the comedy Vacation, and the black comedy horror film Arizona. Gillies debuted on Victorious with the song "Give It Up" in 2011, singing with Ariana Grande.
