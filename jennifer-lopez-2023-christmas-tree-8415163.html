Jennifer Lopez's 2023 Christmas Tree Revealed

Publish date: 2024-02-28

Jennifer Lopez is decking the halls!

The actress and singer, 54, unveiled her 2023 Christmas tree on Tuesday with a series of photos on Instagram — and let the snaps speak for themselves.

In the pictures, the “On The Floor” singer posed in front of the festive evergreen, which was adorned with frosty pine cones, warm-toned string lights and a variety of gold ornaments.

Lopez, who takes Christmas decorating quite seriously (last year she even set a holiday theme: “hummingbird”), kept the color scheme going at the base of the towering tree with presents wrapped in gold paper and decorated with pinecones.

Like the presents, which perfectly complemented the spruce’s color-coordinated decor, the actress also matched her tree in a warm, metallic ensemble — down to her shoes.

Several stars complimented Lopez and her tree, including Paris Hilton, who wrote, “Stunning 😍,” and Thalía, who commented, “🥰👏🏼🥰.”

For the festive debut, the Marry Me star snapped the photos from her Beverly Hills home, which she shares with husband Ben Affleck.

Lopez and Affleck, 51, purchased the $61 million property together this spring after a long house hunt, The Wall Street Journal reported at the time.

In the months leading up to the purchase, multiple reports circulated about the couple nearly closing on several high-ticket homes, but the pair ultimately selected the massive California mansion.

The sprawling property boasts 12 bedrooms and 24 bathrooms, as well as an outdoor sports complex with both basketball and pickleball facilities.

The couple’s big purchase came months after the Hustlers star put her Bel Air mansion on the market for $42.5 million in February 2023.

Her former gated, eight-acre estate included many amenities, including nine bedrooms, 12 bathrooms and a home theater — which was lined with posters from both Lopez and Affleck's movies, the real estate images showed.

Affleck also put his former Los Angeles home on the market earlier this year.

In August, the Air director listed his Pacific Palisades home for $30 million. The 13,500-square-foot L.A. property features seven bedrooms and nine bathrooms, and is located near the posh Riviera Country Club.

Affleck and Lopez first rekindled their romance in 2021, got engaged in April 2022 and tied the knot in Las Vegas in July 2022. The movie stars also celebrated their marriage in a ceremony at Affleck’s Georgia home in August 2022.

Last year, for their first Christmas as a married couple, Lopez penned a sweet message about their blended family along with her announcement about her “hummingbird” theme. (Lopez shares 15-year-old twins, Max and Emme, with ex-husband Marc Anthony and Affleck shares kids Violet, 18, Seraphina, 14, and Samuel, 11, with ex-wife Jennifer Garner.)

In her On the JLo newsletter, the star said, “I decided this year that the hummingbird would be a perfect theme,” which she carried out on her tree with a combination of teal ornaments and hummingbirds in both silver and gold.

"We have blended families, doubled the fun, doubled the love, doubled the presents and triple the chaos!!" she added.
