Tallia Storm rocks glam plunging dress for luxurious Cartier event

Publish date: 2024-04-06

TALLIA Storm stuns in low-cut dress at sparkling Cartier Polo match.

The Scottish singer, who is no stranger to rocking designer brands, glammed up for a luxurious polo match hosted by famous French Jewellery brand, Cartier.

Tallia, 24, shared a glance into her glittering day out with her 550K Instagram followers.

Posting to her story, Tallia shared a snap of her invitation to the Cartier Queen's Cup, writing alongside the image: "So excited for today!"

In the picture, the blonde beauty appeared to have been chauffeured in a leather-interior car.

Tallia could be seen posing in a clip to follow, rocking a stunning plunging gown, detailed with black bows.

She paired the look with a sultry up-do and a radiant full face of makeup, including a classic nude lip.

The eye-popping pale pink gown flaunted Tallia's glowing skin and featured elegant ruffles, ideal for the fashionable day out.

Giving her social media fans a better look into the opulent setting, the Capital Breakfast presenter filmed a video panning over the outdoor event.

The set-up included a lush green lawn, huddles of guests, chic floral arrangements and an array of white gazebos scattered with plush seating areas.

Tallia even zoomed in on a line-up of dapperly dressed waiters, offering a variety of delicious looking cocktails available to the guests.

Sending pulses racing, the musician could be seen posing against a beautiful backdrop of flowers, decorated with a golden sign stating: "Cartier".

Working her angles expertly, the star looked amazing as she put her floor-length frock on display.

The lavish invitation comes after Tallia exclusively told The Sun that she had previously "looked rich" on social media despite being "skint".

She stated: "I was at Cannes this year but this time last year, I literally could not afford my rent. However, there was a buzz about me [in 2022] because I was on the red carpet at the Brit Awards.

"But I couldn't afford to live in London. It's so expensive. So I moved in with my sister who is a year and a half older than me."
