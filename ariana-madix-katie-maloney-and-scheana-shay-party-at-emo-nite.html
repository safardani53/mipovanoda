Ariana Madix, Katie Maloney and Scheana Shay Party at Emo Nite

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Girls night out! Ariana Madix was joined by Katie Maloney and Scheana Shay at Emo Nite in Los Angeles on Friday, April 7, one month after Scandoval was revealed.

The Vanderpump Rules stars joined event founder T.J. Petracca in matching black “Every Nite Is VPR Nite” hoodies. “Holy Emo … this night was a trip. And also an honor thank you @emonite for an incredible night! Let’s do it again like so many more times,” Maloney, 36, captioned photos via Instagram.

Shay, 37, shared her own post, writing, “Watch out James Kennedy, there’s a new DJ in town 🖤 @emonite.”

Shay’s husband, Brock Davies, was also along for the fun night out. The ladies started their night with drinks and dinner at SugarTaco, where they enjoyed spicy watermelon margaritas (dubbed the “Revenge Dress”) before they headed to Emo Nite venue, Avalon.

Once in the club, the ladies hit the DJ booth, jamming out to Paramore, My Chemical Romance, All American Rejects and more punk pop hits. At one point, Shay even led the crowd in a “F–k Tom” chant, per social media footage.

Mod Sun, Fefe Dobson and Simple Plan drummer Chuck Comeau also stopped by to rock out at the event.

The night out came just days after Madix, 37, released a new line of Team Ariana merchandise. The official shop includes clothing, drink ware and accessories that read “It’s not me, it’s you,” “Bad Bitches Only” and “Best F–king Friend.”

Madix split from Tom Sandoval, ending their 9-year romance, in early March after learning of his affair with Vanderpump Rules costar Raquel Leviss. The trio confronted each other at the season 10 reunion, which was taped on March 23. After the dramatic showdown, Madix leaned on her VPR girls.

She joined Shay and Maloney as well as past castmates Kristen Doute and Dayna Kathan for drinks after wrapping the special. In a clip shared via social media from the outing, Kathan, 32, shared footage of the group booing as the bar played news coverage of Scandoval.

“Any questions?” she wrote alongside the video of Ariana’s friends making aggressive thumbs down motions with photos of Sandoval, 40, and Raquel, 28, splashed across the screens.

Both the restauranteur and former pageant queen apologized to Ariana via statements on social media, but the status of their relationship after their infidelity was made public is unclear.
