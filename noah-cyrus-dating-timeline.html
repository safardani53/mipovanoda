Noah Cyrus's Dating Timeline: Singer's Most Notable Romances

Publish date: 2024-04-29

Here's a comprehensive Noah Cyrus dating timeline — from her romance with Lil Xan to her recent engagement. We have all of the details.

While Noah Cyrus may have spent the early years of her career in the shadow of her older sister, Miley Cyrus, the singer/actor has proven her talents with a Grammy Award nomination. The "Lonely" singer secured her first Grammy nod in the Best New Artist category, alongside the likes of Doja Cat, Megan Thee Stallion, and D Smoke.

Aside from her singing and songwriting, Noah has made headlines for her risk-taking fashion, her emotional lyrics, and her past high-profile relationships.

From those infamous photos with Lil Xan to her fleeting romance with Demi Lovato, we have a refresher on the Ponyo actor's dating timeline. Keep reading to learn more about her relationship history.

Noah Cyrus has a song (and a tattoo) with her ex, Tanner Drayton.

The artist's first public relationship appears to have been with musician Tanner Drayton, who goes by the stage name Tanner Alexander. 

The couple released a song together called "Lately" in 2018, and they got tattoos together in June of that year.

Celebrity tattoo artist JonBoy worked with Noah and Tanner, and they each got five stars tattooed (four of them were filled in) on their lower arms. 

The two split shortly after getting matching ink, further proving that getting couple tattoos is the kiss of death.

Noah Cyrus dated rapper Lil Xan in 2018.

Noah's most infamous relationship to date was with rapper Lil Xan (real name Diego Leanos). The two started dating in June 2018, when Lil Xan sent a DM to Noah asking to hang out. Their romance began shortly after "The End of Everything" artist ended her relationship with Tanner. 

When the duo attended the 2018 MTV VMAs together, they took a few photos on the red carpet — and they soon went viral.

Despite publicly professing their love for one another on social media, Noah and Lil Xan split by September 2018. They each took to their respective Instagram stories to discuss the breakup, and things got messy fast.

They both accused the other of cheating, and Lil Xan accused Noah of faking their relationship.

Lil Xan later apologized to Noah and the rest of the Cyrus family for the breakup. He began dating YouTuber Gabby Parsons in November 2019, but they broke up in the summer of 2020 because of his reported infidelity.

He did appear in public with Noah again in April 2020, but it the two didn't reignite their relationship.

Noah Cyrus *may* have dated DJ Diablo too.

The former Hannah Montana guest star dated another heavily tattooed music star in the fall of 2019. Diablo, who is a DJ, confirmed his relationship with Noah with an Instagram post. In one of the photos he put up, he can be seen giving Noah a kiss on the lips.

Noah never confirmed that she was dating Diablo, but she did post about him being a good friend.

Noah Cyrus was spotted getting cozy with Machine Gun Kelly at the 2020 Grammy Awards.

During an after-party for the Grammy Awards in January 2020, onlookers noticed that Noah was holding hands with Machine Gun Kelly.

The two had previously interacted with one another online, and she appeared in the music video for his 2019 song, "I Think I'm Okay," but it was the first time that they had been linked romantically.

Noah Cyrus was then linked to singer Demi Lovato.

Noah Cyrus announced her engagement to Pinkus in June 2023.

In April 2023, Noah introduced the world to her relationship with a fashion designer, Pinkus. It was only two months later, in June 2023, that she took to Instagram to tell us that they were engaged!
