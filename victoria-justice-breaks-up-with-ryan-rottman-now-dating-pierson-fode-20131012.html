Victoria Justice Breaks Up With Ryan Rottman, Now Dating Pierson Fode

Publish date: 2024-03-05

There’s a new man in Victoria Justice‘s life! Multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly that the 20-year-old Nickelodeon star has split from boyfriend Ryan Rottman — and is already dating someone else. See more pics of Justice here.

“It happened awhile ago,” an insider tells Us of Justice’s break up with Rottman, 29. “They are at different points in their life and both had been traveling. They’ve both moved on.”

And it doesn’t sound like there are any hard feeling between the Victorious actress and Lying Game actor, who first began dating back in 2011.

“Ryan is working on Happyland with MTV and Victoria just finished another show [Eye Candy] for MTV, so they’re still on the same network,” the insider explained. “They are both seeing other people now.”

Indeed, a second source tells Us the singer and actress is now dating her Naomi and Ely’s No Kiss List costar Pierson Fode. The two were recently spotted getting cozy at the Boohoo.com viewing party at the Staples Center’s Hyde Lounge for Beyonce‘s Mrs. Carter Show World Tour and after that they hit Bootsy Bellows until late.
