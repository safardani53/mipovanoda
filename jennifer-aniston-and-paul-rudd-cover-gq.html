Jennifer Aniston and Paul Rudd Cover GQ

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Wanderlust co-stars Jennifer Aniston and Paul Rudd cover GQ this month.

It’s not quite as racy as the infamous Jennifer Aniston nude GQ cover from 2008, but the photos, shot by Terry Richardson, show the co-stars in some sexy poses.

Jen has no shirt on and appears to be choking Paul, for example:

Aniston also took the time to talk babies, her boyfriend (Justin Theroux) and marriage in her GQ interview. On the non-stop pregnancy rumors, she says:

“I’m not having triplets. Or twins. Nor am I having one baby. [lifts up sweater, gestures to flat belly] I did not elope. You heard it here. I’m not knocked up.”

As for turning 40 (she’s 43 as of Saturday), the actress says she could not be happier: “Forty’s great! Oh, my God, my thirties blew! My forties are great.”

Certainly a positive attitude.

 Paul and Jen both talked about how their new film resonates. He says:  “I think most marriages, you’re constantly tending the garden, constantly working at it.”

Jen adds: “It’s a growing, literally living thing. There’s going to be growing pains, there’s going to be awkward moments, your lanky phase, your pimpled phase.”

“I think that’s the beauty of a relationship. But if the road starts to diverge and one person is growing this way and the other is growing that way… Sometimes that can happen and you can stay together, and sometimes you can’t.”
