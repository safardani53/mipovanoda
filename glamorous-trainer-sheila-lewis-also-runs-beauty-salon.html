Glamorous trainer Sheila Lewis also runs beauty salon but after 1,132-1 treble it's punters who wax

Publish date: 2024-06-02

SHEILA LEWIS knows a thing or two about sorting the foul from the foals.

The part time beautician, part time trainer is having a big impact on the racing world this season.

Having not won more than twice a year until October she landed a stunning 1,182-1 treble at Hereford to put her name up in highlights and now has nine winners for the year.

In October she said: “It's absolutely amazing.

"We've only got a small yard and we're happy to get two winners a year let alone three in a day.”

She trains eight horses in the village of Three Cocks near Brecon in the Welsh hills each morning, running her beauty salon in the afternoons.

But she thinks spending more time with her horses during lockdown helped her get into the groove and pick up some impressive wins.

She told the Mail on Sunday: “I’ve juggled the two jobs for five years. It has been really tough because they're completely opposite.

"I'm out with the horses then I have to get my act together and get my glam kit on for the salon.

“In March we were locked down so I had to close the salon. I concentrated on the horses and threw myself into the training. I think there's definitely a connection.
