Vanderpump Rules' Ariana Madix smiles ear to ear as she finally moves out of $2M LA home she shared

Publish date: 2024-08-24

VANDERPUMP Rules star Ariana Madix was all smiles as she packed up her things and moved out of the house she shared with her ex, Tom Sandoval.

Ariana, 37, was photographed carrying many boxes to a very full moving van and posted a video on social media.

The Vanderpump Rules star wore a blue sweatshirt, black pants, and white shoes to move out of the Los Angeles home she previously shared with co-star and boyfriend of 9 years, Tom, 39.

About 2 months ago, Ariana discovered that Tom had an affair with her co-star and best friend, Raquel Leviss, 28.

She also shared a video on her Instagram Story. The TV personality smiled big and gave fans a look at her packed bags.

She captioned her video "ready to dip out" with a smirking emoji.

MORE ABOUT THE CHEATING SCANDAL

In March, TMZ first reported that Tom and Ariana broke up amid claims that he was unfaithful.

Sources from production claimed Ariana called it quits shortly after discovering the alleged affair.

Producers reportedly filmed the aftermath of the cheating scandal and planned to include the spicy drama in Season 10.

That same month, sources told Page Six that Tom and Raquel had their affair for months before the cheating scandal broke.

The same insider also told the outlet the two have decided they “want to be together.”

Tom and Raquel reportedly "disappeared for hours" during co-star Scheana Shay's wedding in August 2022 before they were caught kissing ahead of the ceremony, The U.S. Sun exclusively reported.

MOVING ON

Although Raquel reportedly dumped her co-star, according to Page Six, Ariana denied the recent reports that the twosome split on Watch What Happens Live with host Andy Cohen.

She claimed: “I don’t buy that at all she was sending letters to my house like four days ago."

Meanwhile, Tom's ex appears to have moved on with her new man, Daniel Wai, which The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed.

SHE'LL BE BACK

Last week, Ariana was a special guest on the Today show.

She spoke with Hoda Kotb and Jenna Bush Hager about her time on the show.

Hoda asked Ariana: "So we saw the end of this show, we watched the finale. I was thinking at the end, I wonder, if you would consider going back again for another season or if you've had your fill of reality TV."

Ariana replied: "Um…I think I'll be back."

She laughed before adding: "If they'll have me back!"

Jenna said: "I think they'll have you back."

The women laughed, especially since Ariana has been such a popular part of the show as a whole.

Hoda asked if she has any rules about whether Tom or Raquel couldn't be back if she returned or anything like that.

Ariana was clear about saying she doesn't have any control over that.

"I have no interest in speaking to either of them. I don't have anything to say."

She added that she didn't plan on being in the same room, either.
