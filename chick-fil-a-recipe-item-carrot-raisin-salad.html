Chick-fil-A shares special 10-year-old recipe after fans beg the chain to bring back the popular dis

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Chick-fil-A has revealed a recipe for a popular item that it once had on its menus.

For decades, customers at the fast food chain were able to order the carrot & raisin salad.

But in 2015, Chick-fil-A confirmed it was officially axed from the menu.

But that hasn't stopped fans from urging Chick-fil-A to bring the salad back.

One said in an X post: "I love @ChickfilA but am frustrated they keep taking away my favorites! No more Fudge brownie, Carrot and Raisin salad, Coleslaw, and now the cow calendar."

Another wrote on X: "I dearly miss @ChickfilA carrot+ raisin salad."

And a third asked: "Can you bring back the carrot and raisin salad? We are all missing it!"

In multiple X posts in response to fans, Chick-fil-A has said that it does not "have plans to bring back" the salad.

THE RECIPE

However, it has provided a recipe so diehards can make it at home.

To make it, you'll need to mix together the following list of ingredients

  • Half a cup of mayonnaise
  • Half a cup and two tablespoons of sugar
  • One and a quarter cup of unstrained lemon juice with no seeds
  • Three-quarters of a cup of raisins
  • 4.5 cups of shredded carrots
  • An eight-ounce can or two-thirds of a cup of crushed, unstrained pineapple

The recipe is enough for between 13 and 14 servings.

But those who want to buy the item still can find them at Truett’s Grill and Dwarf House restaurants in Atlanta, Georgia.

MORE CHICK-FIL-A NEWS

Meanwhile, Chick-fil-A has been busy making changes.

One includes making a big change to its app.

Customers can now customize orders by adding ingredients for entrees, snacks, and sides.

Some new options customers will come across include strawberry lemonade, chocolate syrup for cookies & cream milkshakes, and hash brown scramble bowl with either spicy chicken or large hashbrowns.

On the downside of the news, Chick-fil-A warned that customers won't be able to "alter cooking times" through the app for any items, and "certain" add-ons will cost extra.

The company is transitioning" into providing what it's calling a "double-wall paper cup."

It is meant to boost customer experience with its insulated sleeve and "lid fit and performance," according to Chick-fil-A.

Additionally, Chick-fil-A is adding the honey pepper pimento chicken sandwich and the caramel crumble milkshake.

They will join the menu on August 28.
