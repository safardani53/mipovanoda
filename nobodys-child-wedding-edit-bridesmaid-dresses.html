Nobody's Child launches wedding edit and it's perfect for summer

Publish date: 0001-01-01

NOBODY'S CHILD's new wedding edit is perfect for the summer season.

For the brand's first foray into bridal wear, the responsibly made bridesmaids dresses are designed to be reworn again and again.

There are 16 dresses in the collection, in varying styles ranging from capped sleeves to cowl neck or sleeveless designs.

Each design comes in sizes UK4 to UK18 and there are a range of wedding appropriate shades to choose from.

Think softs pastels, pretty blues and flattering peaches.

Despite being perfect bridesmaids dresses, the styles are also beautiful for any formal summer occassion, meaning you can easily rewear it long after the wedding day has passed.

These are some of our favourites from the collection.

Lilac Frances Midaxi Bridesmaid Dress

This flattering lilac satin gown has a pretty high neck which creates a long, elegant shape.

Coming down to mid-shin, it is the perfect length for weddings and light and airy for summer.

  • Lilac Frances Midaxi Bridesmaid Dress, £75 - buy here

Blue Bridesmaids Erin Midaxi Dress

Coming in a beautiful baby blue, this midaxi dress feels like Ally's dress in The Notebook - 1940s, classy and demure.

Cap sleeves are particularly good for weddings in churches or other religious buildings and this is both formal enough for bridesmaids while still being perfect for other summery events.

  • Blue Bridesmaids Erin Midaxi Dress, £85 - buy here

Peach Trixie Midaxi Bridesmaid Dress

This cowl neck option has slinky, barely there straps and a simple design.

It looks particularly good paired with strappy heels or wedges for any time of year.

  • Peach Trixie Midaxi Bridesmaid Dress, £75 - buy here

Petite Lilac Coco Midi Dress

For a non-satin option, this ruffled lilac dress comes down to the ankle with a tasteful split to just over the knee.

The ruffles adorn the front and back of this dress and its eye catching without being ostentatious.

  • Petite Lilac Coco Midi Dress, £95 - buy here

Olive Green Bridesmaid Trixie Midaxi Dress

This gorgeous olive green midaxi dress is the same design as the earlier peach colour, but we think this one's beautiful for an autumn wedding.

Tapping into the earthiness of the later part of the year, it's a seriously flattering colour on a super pretty design.

  • Olive Green Bridesmaid Trixie Midaxi Dress, £75 - buy here

Sky Blue Dove Midi Dress

Floaty and romantic, the empire waistline allows for a loose skirt which is particularly good for summer while the texture around the sleeves and chest add some interest.

Flattering and comfortable, this is an ideal summer dress.

  • Sky Blue Dove Midi Dress, £95 - buy here
