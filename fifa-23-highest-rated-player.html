Who is the highest rated player on FIFA 23? its not Mbapp, Messi or Benzema

Publish date: 2024-09-03

FIFA 23’s Ultimate Edition launches tomorrow, and developer EA Sports has revealed who its highest rated player is ahead of release.

However, FIFA while fans were expecting popular players such as cover star Kylian Mbappé, Lionel Messi, Karim Benzema, or Kevin De Bruyne, they were surprised to find out who took the top spot.

These men are all excellent footballers, and their 91-rating in FIFA 23 shows how great they are at the sport.

Who is FIFA 23's highest rated player?

Only one player has FIFA's highest rating on 92.

Barcelona’s midfielder, Alexia Putellas, has taken the top spot as the world’s best footballer.

She also captains the Spanish national team where she has seen great success.

More in FIFA 23

Putellas is certainly deserving of the title of FIFA 23’s best player. 

Last year she won the Ballon d’Or Féminin, the FIFA best women’s player, and the UEFA Women’s Player of the Year, the only person to win all three in the same year.

Who has a 90+ rating in FIFA 23?

A number of women players also match Mbappé and co, with a player rating of 91.

FIFA cover star, Sam Kerr, has been captain of Australia’s national side since 2019.

She’s also Chelsea’s star forward, earning her a 91-rating.

France not only has Mbappé, and Benzema taking the top spots, but two women players as well.

Wendie Renard, captain of the French national side, and club Lyon rates at 91.

Another player for Lyon, Ada Hegerberg, also managed to snag a 91-rating.

While fewer female players rated 90+ compared to their male counterparts this year, there are still more women taking the top spots.

Four more female players were given a rating of 90, including former FIFA 15 cover star Alex Morgan.

Six male players have a rating of 90 including Ronaldo and Mo Salah.
