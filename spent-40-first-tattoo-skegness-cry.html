I spent 40 on my first tattoo in Skegness - it looks nothing like I wanted, I could cry

Publish date: 2024-10-01

GETTING a tattoo can be a HUGE decision, because that ink is for life.

But when Analise Taylor from Nottinghmam went all the way to Skegness to spend £40 on her very first one, she was left majorly disappointed at the result.

In a TikTok, she showed the tattoo she wanted, vs the tattoo she actually got.

The first image shows a perfect circle with sun strands illustrated around it.

But the second photo showed Analise's tattoo, which has a wonky circle and dropping sun strands drawn around it.

Analise said the tattoo artist traced her image from a photo of a phone screen.

She named the tattoo shop in the comments and warned others not to go.

She captioned her tattoo video with: "He 'fixed it' after but Skegness ay… great first tattoo experience," rather sarcastically as her sadness at the finished outcome was obvious.

Others flocked to offer her reassurance and kind words as one commented: "Mine looked a bit wonky when I had mine might just be areas of swelling just wait for it to heal then see what it looks like."

To which Analise replied: "Yeah he fixed some lines after but it’s really just oval instead of circle but isn’t as bad as that photo now - hopefully it will be ok.

"Oh dear I’m sorry ur first tattoo as welll," said a commenter who felt bad for her.

While one observed: "Ermmmmmm bit wonky init."

"Omg what a proper tattooist would have drawn up their own similar version! Hope ur okay," wrote another, offering their condolences and providing some insight on what a real tattoo artist normally does.

As a solution for the bad tattoo, Analise said she: "Can’t get over it."

She explained: "We went back and tried to “fix it” but it’s still so wonky but my tattoo artist I trust is going to try and sort it in my next appointment," so hopefully she gets an ink she likes, eventually.
