Chipotle makes major change to its rewards program and customers will love the 'food drops'

Publish date: 2024-03-02

GET lots of bang for your buck at Chipotle in 2023, which has announced several free food deals for rewards members and other customers across North America. 

On Monday, Chipotle Mexican Grill launched its “Freepotle” program that is set to give out ten free “food drops” to rewards members throughout the year. 

The food drops will provide customers with a variety of free items including free guac, Queso Blanco, beverages, chips, and double protein. 

WHAT IS FREEPOTLE?

Freepotle is a rewards-member-only program that will provide multiple opportunities for customers in 2023. 

Current Chipotle rewards members will automatically be enrolled into the perk, the Mexican grill announced.

Non-members interested in gaining access to the freebies can also be enrolled by joining the restaurant’s rewards program by March 6.

Customers should start seeing perks immediately – getting access to the first “food drop” of the year on January 9. 

Available until January 15, customers have been given a one-time free topping or side of guac with a purchase of $5.00 or more. 

The “food drop” will expire if not used within seven days of being added to the rewards account. 

Chipotle has warned that, like many promotions, the food drop cannot be combined with other offers or coupons and is subject to product availability at the time of redemption.

New Chipotle rewards members that enroll into the program before March 6 will also get the free side or topping of guac. 

OTHER OFFERS

In addition to the ten food drops in 2023, Freepotle is also giving Chipotle lovers a chance to win free food from the burrito hot spot for one year at all restaurants in the US and Canada. 

Rewards members have from January 9 to January 15 to enter into the drawing. 

To be eligible for the free Chipotle, members must make a $5 minimum purchase and scan their rewards member ID at the register. 

The purchase will automatically enter them into the drawing. 

Members can also enter by making a $5 minimum purchase on the app or website when signed into their account. 

According to the company, fans are permitted to enter once a day over the seven day entering period. 

Chipotle is expected to give out approximately 3,100 “free Chipotle For a Year” prizes, one for each of Chipotle’s locations. 

Additionally, the company is launching the “Freepotle” Prize Wheel on TikTok and Instagram live. 

From January 9 to January 13, Chipotle will give two viewers free Chipotle for a year. 

The drawings will take place at 9am PT/12pm ET. 

Burrito lovers can enter by watching the live videos and commenting “Freepotle.” 

WHY JOIN?

Through Freepotle, the Mexican Grill views their rewards program a “must-join program” for all their customers, according to Chris Brandt, chief marketing officer. 

Brandt touted the program as a way to provide affordable food to the communities they are a part of. 

“In an environment full of pricey subscription programs, we're introducing a pass to our real food that is free to join and will provide more value to our community than ever before," he said. 

HOW TO JOIN

Creating a Chipotle rewards account is fairly simple. 

All users have to do is go to the restaurant's website or app and click on the top menu option to sign in. 

Once there, users can create a free account using a phone number and email address.
