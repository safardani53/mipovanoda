JoJo Fletcher Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Bio:
JoJo Fletcher (born Joelle Fletcher in Dallas, Texas on November 25, 1990) was the runner-up on Season 20 of The Bachelor and is currently the Season 12 Bachelorette. Prior to that, she graduated from Baylor University in Waco, Texas with a BA in Applied Science degree in 2011; she actually started her own real estate development company, JHF Realty Development.

Best Known For:
JoJo Fletcher is best known for starring on season 20 of The Bachelor and season 12 of The Bachelorette.

Personal Life:
JoJo was dumped by Ben Higgins during the Bachelor finale, which aired on March 14, 2016. She got engaged to Jordan Rodgers on season 12 of The Bachelorette. She is not the only one in her family who is a reality star. Her brother was on Ready for Love, and JoJo was seen on an episode when he brought home ladies to meet his family. Her brother also went to school with ex-Bachelor, Jake Pevelka.
