Joe Dumars Biography

Joe Dumars was born on a Friday, May 24, 1963 in Shreveport. His birth name is Joe Dumars III and he is currently 60 years old. People born on May 24 fall under the zodiac sign of Gemini. His zodiac animal is Rabbit.

Joe Dumars III is a retired American basketball player in the National Basketball Association. At 6’3″ Dumars could play either shooting guard or point guard on offense and was a highly effective defender. He played for the Detroit Pistons from 1985 until 1999. During the late 1980s and early 1990s, Dumars and Isiah Thomas combined to form one of the best backcourts in NBA history. Initially a shooting guard, Dumars moved to point guard following Thomas’ retirement in 1994, sharing ball-handling duties with Grant Hill. Dumars was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame in 2006. Dumars served as the President of Basketball Operations for the Detroit Pistons from 2000 to 2014.

He played college basketball at McNeese State from 1981 to 1985 and was selected 18th overall in the 1985 NBA Draft.
