Anna Faris PICS Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-02-14

Anna Faris, born November 29, 1976, is an American actress, podcaster and author. She is known for her comedic roles in films such as the Scary Movie films (2000–2006), The Hot Chick (2002), Just Friends (2005), The House Bunny (2008), and What’s Your Number? (2011). Other notable films she’s starred in include,  My Super Ex-Girlfriend (2006), Smiley Face (2007), Lost in Translation (2003), The Dictator (2012), and Overboard (2018). She’s also had voice-over roles in the film franchises Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs (2009–2013) and Alvin and the Chipmunks (2009–2015), as well as The Emoji Movie (2017). Anna had a reoccurring role in the tenth and final season of Friends (2004). She’s played the co-lead role on the CBS sitcom Mom since 2013. In 2015, she launched Unqualified, an advice podcast, and in 2017, her memoir of the same name was released. Anna was married to actor, Chris Pratt from 2009 until their August 2017 split. The pair, who share a son, Jack, finalized their divorce in October 2018. Before Chris, she was was married to Ben Indra from 2004 to 2008. Anna began dating cinematographer, Michael Barrett in November 2017. She was photographed wearing an engagement ring on November 3, 2019.
