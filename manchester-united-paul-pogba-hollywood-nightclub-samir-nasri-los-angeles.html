Publish date: 2024-04-30

PAUL POGBA was reduced to a mere mortal as he was made to wait in a queue for Hollywood hotspot Warwick.

And just behind the world record Manchester United star stood another footballer - Manchester City exile Samir Nasri.

Pogba may command a salary of nearly £300,000-a-week at Old Trafford after his £89MILLION move from Juventus last summer, but that doesn't mean he can get a queue jump at Warwick nightclub.

The celebrity hangout has become one of the most popular venues on the Los Angeles party scene in recent years, with Taylor Swift, Jennifer Aniston and Nicole Scherzinger all visiting in recent times.

The venue, where 3l Jeroboams of Dom Perginon can cost more than £6,000 ($8,000), has become a favourite of Rita Ora, who has visited multiple times.

Pogba is currently on holiday in the US - where he has spent many summers.

The French midfielder, 24, also met up with Everton star Romelu Lukaku and was seen playing with a ball around a swimming pool.

Pogba has spent the summer travelling the world and has already been to Mecca, Saudi Arabia, and China.

He went to Mecca for Ramadan, dabbed on the Great Wall of China and filmed rappers in Cartagena, Colombia, a World Heritage site.

Samir Nasri's summer has been slightly less enjoyable, even though he's also travelled.

The French midfielder, who spent last season on loan at Sevilla is facing a doping BAN.

Manchester City fear being lumbered with a £12.5m bill as Nasri faces a two-year ban.

The probe comes after Nasri visited the Drip Doctors clinic in Los Angeles last year - that sparked a huge spat when former girlfriend Anara Atanes hacked his social media and accused him of cheating.

Under World Anti-Doping Agency regulations such sessions are banned in quantities of no greater than 50millilitres in a six hour time-frame – or unless clearance has been given on medical grounds.

Nasri was seen looking excited earlier this month with model girlfriend Sharina Gutierrez at a water park in LA.

He also posted a photo alongside Arsenal and Crystal Palace flop Marouane Chamakh at an unknown beach location.

Pogba's Manchester United team-mate Marcus Rashford is also in the US - at the Nike World Campus in Portland, Oregon.

Although the likes of Zlatan Ibrahimovic, Luke Shaw and Ashley Young are already back in training, Jose Mourinho is not expecting the rest of his stars to return until July 8.

With United travelling to the US for a five-game pre-season tour, it is not yet clear if Pogba will return to Manchester or will meet the squad in America.
