Met Gala 2016 Red Carpet: Zoe Saldana Kisses Marco Perego

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Seal it with a kiss! Zoe Saldana stunned in a dramatic Dolce & Gabbana gown with a long train on the red carpet at the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Costume Institute Gala in New York City on Monday, May 2.

The Avatar actress, 37, arrived to the fashion event in an absolutely gorgeous multi-colored dress that featured a strapless black corset bodice and a long, feathered train that trailed behind her as she walked up the Met’s steps.

Saldana brought along her hunky Italian artist husband, Marco Perego, with whom she proudly shared a romantic smooch in front of flashing cameras.

The Colombiana star last attended the Met Gala in 2014, when the theme was Charles James: Beyond Fashion, for which she wore a black and white Michael Kors halter dress.

This year’s theme is Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology. Cochairs include Vogue editor in chief Anna Wintour, Taylor Swift, Idris Elba and Jonathan Ive.
