The Lions placekicker Michael Badgley has no wife and isn’t married. The 28-year-old native of Summit, New Jersey, Michael, has never revealed too many details about his personal life.

Badgley enrolled at Miami in 2014 and set a school record with 25 made field goals in his sophomore year. Later that season, he earned ACSMA All-ACC Honorable Mention recognition. 

During his senior year, Badgley became Miami’s all-time field goal leader and was named Lou Groza Award semifinalist. Depiste, his collegiate success, Badgley remained undrafted and was later picked by the Colts.
