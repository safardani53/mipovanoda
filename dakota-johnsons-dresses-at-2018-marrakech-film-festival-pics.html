Dakota Johnsons Dresses at 2018 Marrakech Film Festival: Pics

Publish date: 2024-07-02

We can always count on Dakota Johnson to wow Us on the red carpet, but the Suspiria actress was in fine form at the 2018 Marrakech International Film Festival. From Cinderella-worthy gowns to glittery separates, the brunette beauty stunned at the 17th edition of the Moroccan event honoring the best in cinema.

The film festival kicked off on Friday, November 30, and Johnson, who served as a jury member, glittered in a Gucci gown. Working with her longtime stylist Kate Young, she proceeded to stun in series of high-fashion dresses that she tempered with her signature undone hair and rosy makeup.
