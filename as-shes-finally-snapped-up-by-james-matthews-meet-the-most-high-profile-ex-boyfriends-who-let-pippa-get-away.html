The seven ex boyfriends who let Pippa get away

Publish date: 2024-03-24

PIPPA married her prince charming in front of high society’s biggest names.

The friendship between her and James spanned a decade before it turned to matrimony. But the 41-year-old hedge fund manager is not Pippa’s first love.

The stunning brunette, 33 – once considered the most eligible woman in Britain – has had a string of exes.

Meanwhile, her new husband has a much more private history of dating.

Rather than romancing a string of women, he is said to have had his eye on Pippa from the very first day they met.

One source said: “You got the sense James was holding out for The One – and that was Pippa.”

Here, KELLY JENKINS looks back at Pippa’s most high-profile romances as she dazzled among the young, rich and famous.

JJ Jardine Paterson

2004-2007: The university boyfriend

Met while studying at Edinburgh.

Heir to a Hong Kong banking dynasty, the  Old Etonian was friends with Pippa’s flatmates, Earl George Percy and Ted Innes-Ker.

He graduated two years before Pippa and  got a  job in  finance.

Billy More Nisbett

2008: Post uni fling

Prince William’s ex-Eton classmate is in property and  loves country pursuits.

He and Pippa met  when they ran in the same Sloaney circles.

They remain friends and were spotted  on a hunt together in 2013.

Married to New Zealand socialite Charlotte Davidson.

Simon Youngman

2008: Post uni fling

After graduation, Pippa  had flings with several eligible bachelors while living in London.

The same year,  she  dated  London socialite Simon, who is heir to a diamond fortune.

He is now the operations manager of high-end  events management company TableTalk.

Alexander Spencer Churchill

2009-2010: Dashing entrepreneur

Met while holidaying in Mustique.

He is the  founder of a wealth management firm  and distantly related to Winston Churchill.

Briefly linked to Tara Palmer-Tomkinson. Married  society beauty Scarlett Strutt.

Alex Loudon

2010-2011: The Royal Wedding date

Alex, a former England cricketer turned City banker, accompanied Pippa to Kate and William’s wedding in  2011.

But soon after, their relationship ended.

Now engaged to his  long-term American  girlfriend Kristina Vanderheyden.

Charlie Gilkes

2011: The rebound

Pippa  hooked up with Old Etonian Charles after her painful split from Alex.

Prince William and Harry’s friend   runs nightclubs and restaurants and was at Pippa’s wedding yesterday,  with his wife, PR  girl Anneke von Trotha Taylor.

Nico Jackson

2012-2015: The one who nearly got her

The stockbroker  stole Pippa’s heart and romanced her for three years,  sparking talk of engagement.

The pair bonded over a mutual love of skiing but he  moved to Switzerland  for work and they struggled with  a long-distance relationship.
