How tall is Outer Banks star Chase Stokes?

Chase Stokes is an actor from Maryland who is best known for his starring role as John B in Outer Banks. 

Stokes started out by landing small roles at the beginning of his career, appearing in Stranger Things, Daytime Divas and The First. 

He originally turned down the offer for Outer Banks but changed his mind. 

John B was his breakout role, he is now adored by fans and his 6.6 million Instagram followers.

When does Outer Banks season three come out?

Outer Banks has officially been renewed for a third season, but no release date is yet available.

Series regulars Chase Stokes, Madelyn Cline, Madison Bailey, Jonathan Daviss, Rudy Pankow, Austin North, Drew Starkey, and Charles Esten will all be returning for the third season.

Season two recurring character Cleo, portrayed by Carlacia Grant, will be returning as a series regular for season three.

Series creator Jonas Pate told Entertainment Weekly in April 2020: “Ever since we started, we always viewed it as something that was probably like a four-season, maybe five-season show, but definitely four seasons.

"We’ve sort of long-arced it out pretty far. I’m just hoping that we get a chance to actually tell those stories.”
