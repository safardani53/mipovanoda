Sofia Richie For OVO Womens FW2020 Campaign: Photos Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Sofia Richie is officially repping October’s Very Own — the partially owned apparel line by none other than Drake. The 22-year-old model is stunning in a series of new campaign photos obtained by HollywoodLife. On December 9, the Toronto-based brand will release its first-ever womenswear online at 12 p.m. local time. The Fall/Winter 2020 collection includes silk sleepwear, edgy streetwear and more cozy essentials with OVO’s signature owl logo, of course.

Ahead of the launch, Sofia, along with the official OVO Instagram account, posted promotional photos of the model sporting the collection. OVO even shared a video montage with behind the scenes footage from Sofia’s photoshoot — which took place inside a modern, California home with a fireplace and cream-colored furniture. Additionally, the OVO account has a highlight reel on Instagram that contains Sofia’s campaign photos, as well as polaroid-style shots from the shoot.

In numerous photos, Sofia models a silk, black button-up pajama set with gold designs. The same print is similarly incorporated throughout a sheer black bodysuit she’s pictured wearing in other snaps. Sofia paired the turtleneck top with black OVO sweats that included a large, white owl logo on the left thigh.

Another look (and our favorite!) includes a cream-colored (almost beige) sweatshirt and matching pants, both of which feature the brand’s owl logo in black and white. In the aforementioned video montage, Sofia is captured running across a couch while sporting the sweat set. It’s the only shot in the campaign that shows her blonde hair styled in an effortless up do’.

Other pieces in the collection include a long sleeve black bodysuit with a turtleneck top and cream-colored logo tees. OVO’s first-ever womenswear will become available on the brand’s website on December 9.
