GOLDEN Bachelor's Gerry Turner has left fans speechless with a new naked photo ahead of next week's first-ever Golden Bachelor finale.

The official Instagram account for The Golden Bachelor shared a new photo of its leading man Gerry, 72, enjoying some alone time.

Gerry was photographed from behind as he looked out at the beautiful scenery of Costa Rica in front of him.

The Bachelor and his final two women - financial services professional, Theresa Nist, 70, and personal trainer Leslie Fhima, 64 - headed to the country ahead of last week's Fantasy Suite episode.

Gerry went shirtless for the snap and was potentially nude as he lounged in a tub with one arm draped over the edge.

Next to him was an empty bathtub, leaving fans on the edges of their seats as to who Gerry may propose to during next week's finale.

While fans have been giving their theories on who Gerry will give his final rose to, viewers will have to wait until next Thursday to find out.

Gerry narrowed down his final two women after saying goodbye to frontrunner Faith Martin, 61, after hometowns.

In a preview for the final episode, Gerry first showed excitement about proposing to the second love of his life.

The clip showed both Theresa and Leslie meeting and talking to Gerry's children as they expressed their love for him.

However, the leading man was then seen with tears streaming down his face as he opened up about breaking one woman's heart.

"Someone is going to get hurt beyond what I can imagine," he said.

Gerry was upset once more as he admitted to possibly making a mistake, calling it a "close second" to losing his late wife, Toni.

"Had I known this would be how much pain I would cause someone, I would have never taken the first step in this journey," he said.

Gerry's quest to find love is almost over as the first Golden Bachelor will make his decision on the finale on November 30.
