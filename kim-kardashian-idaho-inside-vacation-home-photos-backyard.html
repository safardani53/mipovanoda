Kim Kardashian shows off her $5M Idaho mansion in new photos as she celebrates her 'happy place'

Publish date: 2024-02-03

KIM Kardashian has taken to Instagram to show off her $5million Idaho mansion in new photos displaying the expansive property.

The SKIMS founder owns a five-bed, six-bath, property in Harrison with views of Lake Coeur d'Alene which was bought back in November 2020.

Kim, 42, took to Instagram to share photos from what she called her "happy place."

In her first snap, Kim shared a snap of her backyard with the bright afternoon sun shining over the green grass.

Her second pic showed off a sweet beverage topped with whipped cream and marshmallows which she enjoyed with her scenic view.

Kim showed off more photos of the beautiful landscape around the property including a dramatic golden sunset over a lake.

LAKE PARADISE

The U.S. Sun previously reported Kim's Idaho vacation property has gotten a new paint job.

Photographs exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun show it has been given a full paint job with Kim opting for a darker shade of brown for a classy, rustic look.

A local source claimed the beauty mogul has replaced all of the granite inside and removed the dated interior featured on an online property listing.

Pictures show there are new trees and greenery at the front of the home to keep it more hidden since Kim purchased the property, as the family often vacations at the home.

The insider said: "She has given it a Kim Kardashian makeover and it now looks more like her main LA house with beige/neutral tones initially inspired by her ex Kanye West.

"The home is in a peaceful community with its own security and the private Gozzer Ranch Golf and Lake Club which the family has been seen at on occasion.

"It has a restaurant and bar, outdoor dining area for members, a private beach, jet skis and paddleboards, and huge inflatable waterslides for the kids to enjoy."

"Everyone is very discreet at Gozzer Ranch and Coeur d'Alene is fast becoming a top spot for celebrities, everyone from Chris Pratt to Justin Bieber visits in the summer months," the source concluded.

Her 4,544 square-foot custom-built estate offers breathtaking lake views and also includes a two-car garage and a separate golf cart garage.

It boasts an open-plan kitchen, dining, and living area leading out to an oversized desk with plenty of room for entertaining.

For the adults, it also offers a large wine cellar in a daylight basement, and an outside patio featuring a spa and a lush lawn.

FAMILY GETAWAY

The Hulu star was photographed on Sunday at another property in the area, situated directly on the water and just seconds from the main house.

She was snapped enjoying an early July 4 vacation with her daughter North West, 10, as the mother and daughter went jetskiing together before she jetted off to The Hamptons for a celebrity party.

The family is known to spend time in Idaho every summer with Kim's mom, Kris Jenner, and her sisters also flying to the vacation spot.

Kim shares North and three more kids: daughter Chicago, five; and sons Saint, seven; and Psalm, four, with her ex-Kanye.

KIM'S TAKEOVER

According to records seen by The U.S. Sun, Kim also owns two portions of land nearby.

One plot, on the same road as the $5million abode, is listed at just under 1.3 acres and has a market value of $241,620.

The other empty plot is worth around $700,000 and is situated closer to Gozzer Lake Ranch and Golf Club.

The club is described online as a "rustic retreat in the heart of Idaho" which encompasses 700 acres of "exquisite natural landscape" and is a place where "family memories will be cherished for generations."
