Homes evacuated after man dies in incident as cops and firefighters probe scene in Partington

Publish date: 2024-07-29

HOMES were evacuated after a man was found dead following an "incident" inside a house.

Police and fire crews scrambled to the scene in Partington, Manchester.

Firefighters are still on the suburban street after arriving just after 10.30am today.

A number of properties were evacuated but details on the incident remain unclear.

The man was pronounced dead at the scene and his death is being treated as "non-suspicious", according to cops.

Police said the man's family has been notified and loved ones are currently being supported by officers.
