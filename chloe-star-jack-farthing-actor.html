Who is Chloe star Jack Farthing?

Publish date: 2024-09-27

JACK Farthing has seen his career soar over the years, thanks to his role in BBC's Poldark.

He's now returned to the Beeb in their new drama, Chloe.

Who is Jack Farthing?

35-year-old Jack was born on October 14, 1985, and raised in North London.

He read History of Art at St Catherine's College, Oxford, before attending London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art

Jack is perhaps best known for playing villain George Warleggan in the BBC One drama series Poldark.

He was on the smash hit BBC show from when it started in 2015 until it ended in 2019.

What TV shows and films has Jack been in?

In film, Jack's other acting credits include the Riot Club, and the 2020 Netflix film Love Wedding Repeat.

He also played Charles, Prince of Wales in the biographical psychological drama Spencer.

On TV Jack played John Lennon in ITV's Cilla.

He has also starred in Blandings, Agatha Christie's Poirot, and the ABC Murders.

Is Jack married and does he have children?

Jack keeps his personal life out of the spotlight, so it is not known if he is married.

The actor's relationship status is also not known.

We also don't know if he has any children.

Who does Jack play in Chloe?

Chloe is a new BBC drama that centres around a woman called Becky who is unhappy with her life.

Becky, still living with her mum and working as a temp, compares herself to the picture-perfect lives on Instagram, compulsively returning to one account: Chloe’s. Becky obsessively watches her seemingly flawless life through social media.

But when Chloe dies suddenly, Becky’s need to find out how and why leads her to assume a new identity and engineer a ‘chance’ meeting with Chloe’s best friend, Livia, and infiltrate Chloe's group of close-knit friends.

In Chloe, Jack plays Chloe's brooding friend Richard, who seems to be taking her death harder than her husband.
