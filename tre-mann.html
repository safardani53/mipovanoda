Tre Mann plays as a point guard for the Oklahoma City Thunder (2022- now) after the team drafted him in the 2021 NBA draft.

He began his high school basketball career at The Villages Charter Schools and was named Daily Commercial All-Area Player of the Year in his senior year of high school.

Considered a five-star recruit by 247Sports & Rivals, Mann played collegiate basketball for Florida for his freshman & sophomore year before going for the NBA draft.

His four-year contract with the City Thunder, signed on August 7, 2021, extends throughout the 2024-25 season. By the end of his rookie deal, he will have career earnings of around $14 million.

Apart from his basketball career, he is engaged with Elissa Desantis, with whom he has been in a relationship since 2020.

Tre Mann’s Family Details!

Tre Mann was born on February 3, 2001, in Gainesville, Florida, to Albert Mann & Tai Bey.

It seems his biological parents are separated. Currently, his father is married to Elizabeth Mann; they tied the knot on April 28, 2022.

His father Albert (b. May 25, 1982), attended Interlachen High School and worked as a furniture sales associate at Biglots, according to his Facebook profile.

Mann grew up with five siblings, namely Nas, Layla, Aniaya, MJ & Christian.

Age, Height & Body Measurements

The Florida-born basketball player is 22 years old as of 2023.

Mann stands 1.90 m (6 feet 3 inches) tall & weighs 178 lbs (80 kg).

The black-haired and dark brown-eyed player has a standing reach of 2.5 m (8 feet 2.5 inches) and a wingspan of 1.93 m (6 feet 4 inches).

High School & Collegiate Basketball

Tre Mann played high school basketball at The Villages Charter Schools and averaged 23.6 points and 4.9 rebounds per game in his senior year.

He chose the University of Florida over Kansas, Tennessee & North Carolina to play collegiate basketball.

Mann played his freshman and sophomore year for Florida and averaged 10.2 points and 3.6 rebounds in 53 games.

NBA Career

The Oklahoma City Thunder selected Mann as the 18th overall pick in the 2021 NBA Draft.

He made his NBA debut on October 20, 2021, against the Utah Jazz, scoring 9 points in the game in which Bojan Bogdanovic scored a game-high of 22 points.

Later that season, on March 21, 2022, the point guard recorded his career-high of 35 points playing against the Boston Celtics.

Throughout the 2021-22 season, he played in a total of 60 games, starting in 26 of them, and averaged 10.4 points.

In the 2022-23 season, Mann scored his season-high 25 points against the Los Angeles Clippers on October 25, 2022.

Net Worth & Contracts

Tre Mann holds an estimated net worth between $2 million-$3 million as of 2023.

Although the rookie is yet to sign any endorsement deals, it is just a matter of time before he starts attracting lucrative deals from the top brands.

After being selected in the first round of the 2021 NBA draft, he signed a four-year rookie scale contract worth $14.05 million with the City Thunder on August 7, 2021.

The deal includes an average annual salary of $3.51 million.

On October 15, 2022, the Thunder exercised a $3.19 million option for the 2023-24 season.

He is also eligible for a rookie extension contract for five years worth $205 million with an average annual salary of $35.31 million.

Engaged With Elissa Desantis

The Oklahoma City Thunders’ point guard is engaged to his soon-to-be wife, Elissa Desantis.
