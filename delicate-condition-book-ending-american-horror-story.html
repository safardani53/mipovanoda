AMERICAN Horror Story's season 12 is based on the horror fiction novel, Delicate Condition by Danielle Valentine.

What is the book Delicate Condition about?

Delicate Condition follows Anna Alcott and her strong desire for a family.

While she and her husband Dexter, are having trouble conceiving, the two ultimately decide to embark on the IVF journey.

Anna must figure out how to balance her increasingly popular life as an actress while painting her regular appointments.

However, throughout her journey things start to take a turn as the people she has trusted with her life are becoming ones she doesn't believe she can trust.

Is American Horror Story: Delicate based on the book Delicate Condition?

American Horror Story: Delicate is, indeed, based on Delicate Conditions.

In fact, this is the first time the anthology series based its season on a novel.

In this series, Anna is played by Emma Roberts, and her husband, Dexter is played by Matt Czuchry.
