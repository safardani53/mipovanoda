RHOAs Kenya Moore is dating handsome Israeli restaurateur Roi Shlomo after nasty divorce from Mar

Publish date: 2024-03-23

REAL Housewives of Atlanta's Kenya Moore has a new handsome man after her nasty divorce from ex Marc Daly.

The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal Kenya, 52, is dating Roi Shlomo, an Israeli entrepreneur from Atlanta who is in his mid-40s.

Roi, who is featured on the new season of RHOA, is the founder of multiple businesses- Kale Me Crazy, Turks & Caicos' Aziza Restaurant & Lounge and ATL's Kanvas Event Space, which just launched last year.

He previously founded Yogli Mogli, a frozen yogurt chain, before selling the company in 2014.

Kale Me Crazy, a superfood café, launched in 2013 in Atlanta and has since expanded to 24 other locations- a West Hollywood, California, location is opening soon.

The franchise's revenue is estimated at $13.4million.

Roi is no stranger to the spotlight. He's been interviewed and featured on local websites, such as Simply Buckhead, and national outlets, such as Modern Luxury Jezebel.

According to an online bio, he's from Israel and loves to travel to countries outside of the United States.

Like Kenya, he's a big proponent of giving back and supporting local charities.

"I was raised by my family to give back. Even when I was young, my parents and I would volunteer and donate food and time in Israel. It was and continues to be a source of joy," he told Best Self Atlanta.

'MR. MOORE'

Bravo fans were first introduced to the entrepreneur when the RHOA trailer for season 15 dropped last month.

The cast features Kenya, Kandi Burruss, Sheree Whitfield, Drew Sidora, Marlo Hampton and Sanya Richards-Ross.

In the trailer, Kenya is seen touching his arm and saying, "You look sexy."

Roi, wearing a fashionable beige jacket, chuckles as he chats with the cast and the girls laugh over Kenya's obvious flirtation.

A happy-looking Kenya then confides to cameras: "We might be looking at another Mr. Moore."

She then is seen showing her FaceTime video with Roi to the cast as they are seated at a restaurant.

Kenya's longtime enemy, Marlo, reveals, "Is that Roi? I went on a date with him before. He popped my cherry."

The TV personality doesn't seem to believe Marlo's claim, so she then pretends to throw up and says, "Sorry, there's some bulls**t that got stuck in my throat."

Kenya stood her ground while promoting the upcoming season.

"It's not true. It's 100 percent not true. She makes up everything," Kenya explained to ET about Marlo's claim.

"You can't believe a word that comes out of her mouth."

RUMORS SWIRL

Back in January, rumors began that Kenya was dating a new man following her divorce from ex Marc Daly.

Love B. Scott reported that Kenya was dating "a rich white man."

The site also notes that she was introduced to him by Monyetta Shaw, who is a friend on the Housewives show.

"Kenya's budding romance is expected to be part of her storyline," the story mentioned at the time.

In her interview with ET this month, the reality star teased her new love interest.

"Roi has been someone that I've known for a while, over a year, as friends and I just think we have a lot in common," she dished.

"We have friends in common, likes in common and, if nothing else, a great friendship."

ONGOING DIVORCE

Kenya has been embroiled in a messy divorce to Marc since filing in May 2021, calling it the "world's longest divorce."

The NYC restaurant owner allegedly has been stalling and took months to even turn in basic documents.

The pair have been secretly feuding as he is trying to take a portion of the home they shared during their marriage despite her owning the property.

He also requested she pay for his legal fees and demanded joint legal custody after agreeing to Kenya having primary custody of their daughter.

Together, they are parents to 4-year-old daughter Brooklyn.

Earlier this year, Kenya revealed that they did not have prenup so divorce proceedings were taking longer than usual.

"I feel confident that it will get resolved soon," she told TMZ.

Marc responded to the case by agreeing to Kenya having primary custody, but he demanded joint legal custody — to allow him to make important decisions in his daughter’s life.

The former couple got married in June 2017 and separated in September 2019.

Real Housewives of Atlanta premieres on May 7 at 8 p.m. ET on Bravo.
