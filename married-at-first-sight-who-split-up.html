Married at First Sight: Who split up?

Publish date: 2024-07-27

MARRIAGE is hard enough without having to say 'I do' to a partner you've never met before the alter.

Married at First Sight's extreme experiment has led to numerous failed marriages, as well as a handful of successful ones.

Who has split up on Married at First Sight?

Here is the full list of couples that have parted ways since their debut on Married at First Sight:

Cortney Hendrix and Jason Carrion 

Season: 1

Cortney Hendrix and Jason Carrion tied the knot in 2014 but ultimately divorced after five years of marriage.

Both Cortney and Jason have since remarried.

Cortney and her partner, Sherm, welcomed a son in October 2021, while Jason is now married to British soap star Roxanne Pallett and the pair also share a son together.

Monet Bell and Vaughn Copeland

Season: 1

Vaughn and Monet were the show's first-ever divorce. 

Both Monet and Vaughn do not have active social media accounts.

Vaughn welcomed a son in 2016 with his girlfriend, Jenny Farber, according to Screen Rant.

Davina Kullar and Sean Varricchio 

Season: 2

Davina Kullar and Sean Varricchio divorced on the decision day due to Sean's commitment issues.

Sean has since remarried and has a baby girl named Ellie.

In 2016, Davina married Donald Davis and in 2019, she gave birth to her first child, Hudson Davis.

Jaclyn Methuen and Ryan Ranellone

Season: 2

It was revealed that the pair had split on season two's six-month check-in special.

In 2019, she and her now-fiance welcomed a daughter, Alaina Rose, and a son, Antonio Paul, in January 2022.

Ryan Ranellone is a reportedly single real estate agent on Long Island.

Jessica Castro and Ryan DeNino

Season: 2

Jessica Castro and Ryan DeNino got divorced in 2015 after she said he threatened to kill her, according to the New York Post.

Since then, Jessica welcomed her first child in February 2021, a son, Sergio Jr, with her longtime boyfriend.

Ryan's relationship status is unknown.

Vanessa Nelson and Tres Russell 

Season: 3

It was revealed that the pair had split during season 3's reunion.

At one point, Vanessa was set to be married to Andre Forbes on the show's spinoff, Married At First Sight: Second Chances. However, she and Andre have since split.

Vanessa and Tres' personal relationships are currently unknown.

Samantha Role and Neil Bowlus

Season: 3

On decision day, Samantha said yes, but Neil wanted a divorce so the pair went their separate ways.

In 2018, Sam remarried, and the pair share three daughters. Their first child, Isabella, was born in September 2018, while her twins, Sienna Reiko and Everly Lara, were born in March 2022.

Neil's dating life is currently unknown.

Ashley Doherty and David Norton
