Love Island fans unite to evict villa 'snake' as show hands power to the public in explosive dumping

Publish date: 2024-05-23

LOVE Island fans are determined to turf villa "snake" Andrew Le Page and the other Casa Amor 'cheats' off the show.

In a surprise twist, producers have handed the power to clear out the cast over to drama-loving viewers.

Iain Stirling announced the move at the end of Friday night's Love Island, saying: "Now it's time for you to have your say.

"Head to the app where you can access voting and vote for your favourite couple. Those with the fewest votes are at risk of being dumped from the island.

"So will it be Andrew and Coco? Billy and Tasha? Dami and Summer? Davide and Ekin-su?

"Deji and Indiay? Jacques and Paige? Jay and Chyna, Josh and Indiyah, or Luca and Gemma?"

Many needed little encouragement to decide that Andrew, who upset both Tasha Ghouri and Coco Lodge, needed to be in the firing line.

One insisted he and his other Casa Amor 'cheats' needed to be at risk, with one declaring: "13 minutes to vote?! Let’s get Andrew OUT."

Another posted: "UK people!!! Vote so that Jacques, Dami and Andrew are vulnerable!!!"

A third insisted: "if ANYONE is voting for dami or andrew you’re done x."

However, he does have his supporters, with one writing: "I’m still supporting Andrew even though he f***ed up he’s reacted based of an emotion. We’re all human and we make mistakes."
