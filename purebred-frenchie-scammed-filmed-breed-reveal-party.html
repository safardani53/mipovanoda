A WOMAN was left baffled when she bought a "purebred" dog but was inundated with comments saying she was scammed.

After her TikTok viewers claimed her dog wasn't a French Bulldog after all, the owner got her pooch a DNA test and decided to throw a "breed reveal party".

Eliza Lynne began her hilarious video with a snap of her pooch, captioned: "Our four-year-old Frenchie has some features that made us question if she was a purebred so we had a 'breed reveal party'.

The one-minute post then runs through a series of family members guessing the breed in a typical baby gender reveal style.

Eliza begins with her mum who said she believes that the pooch is "100 per cent a French Bulldog".

Next up was a bloke called Patrick who thought the mutt, named Olive, was a Dandie Dinmont Terrier.
