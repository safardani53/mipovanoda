It’s only the beginning for The Apprentice gossip, so let’s start the soon-to-be-tired trend with contestant Jennifer Crisafulli, who also happens to be a broker for Douglas Elliman – and a poor one at that.

The chick had sold maybe 5 properties and had 1 current $500,000 listing and two rentals. Then, as you reported, a bunch of new properties magically appeared???all in Trump buildings. But she’s not the only exclusive broker. Notice how on each she’s listed below Ilan Bracha???an EVP who is a big broker in both of those Trump buildings and has been for awhile.
But her listings appear with power broker Ilan Bracha, who of course is using Crisafulli as a marketing prop and sliding her a couple bucks on each sale.
