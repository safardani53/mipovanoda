Career

Bongani Mavuso disclosed on LinkedIn that he worked as a Facilitator at Umgeni Water for almost a year. Following that, he joined Ukhozi FM and started functioning as a Senior Producer and Presenter till his death. In addition, he was on the board of the Gcinamasiko Heritage Trust. He was also a member of the IsiZulu National Language Board’s Advisory Council.

For the last 20 years, he has held the positions of Executive Director and Recording Artist at KaMavuso Verse Entertainment. Since March 2017, Mavuso has served as the Group Chief Executive Officer of KaMavuso Group (Pty) Ltd. According to reports, he was also a published novelist, having written Izimbali ZeMfolozi, Inhlantsi, and The Undying Spirit of a Dead Man.
