Olivia Attwood is getting married to footballer Bradley Dack TOMORROW just days after wild Ibiza

Publish date: 2024-04-04

OLIVIA Attwood is marrying footballer Bradley Dack in a star studded ceremony tomorrow.

The 32-year-old is still posting pictures from her hen-do online, but actually returned to the UK a few days ago.

Love Island star Olivia has been engaged to the Blackburn Rovers player since 2019 and will finally be saying I Do after a series of delays.

Fortunately for fans the whole thing is expected to be filmed for Olivia Marries Her Match, which has been filming the build up to her big day.

An insider said: “Fans probably still think Olivia is away but she’s actually at home getting ready to marry Bradley.

“She’s kept everything underwraps ahead of the big day and was forced to change the venue after showing it on the last series of her show and it suddenly becoming very booked up.

Olivia shared a picture of Bradley in his wedding shoes today and wrote: "Warming up those dancing shoes."

Her close friend Georgia Harrison confirmed the wedding day as she reached out for make-up artist for herself and Nicole Bass tomorrow.

Speaking previously about her big day, Olivia told Heat: “I’m incredibly excited, but there are a lot of nerves there, as well. I’ve been having weird stress dreams about it, where I forget to set my alarm and oversleep on the day of the wedding, or walk down the aisle wearing shoes that are too big and trip.”

Olivia and Bradley were due to tie the knot in 2020, but Covid restrictions put a stop to their plans of having a wedding abroad.

They had plans to walk down the aisle a year later but had to put things on hold, and now they are finally having their dream day this year.

Last month, the star suffered from a nightmare issue with her wedding dress as they began the countdown to their big day.

She told her followers: "I have really been back into my weight training recently, I was on a mission to grow my bum for the wedding/he do....

"It's grown.... but now I can't do up my wedding dress... Stunning."

The Sun has contacted Olivia's rep for comment.
