Bachelorette Hannah Brown Engagement Ring: Details

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Calling all Bachelorette fans: it’s been a wild ride, to say the least. Hannah Brown (a.k.a. Hannah B.) ended her relationship with Jed Wyatt after giving the musician the final rose in the competition. The two were briefly engaged, as seen on season 15 of the ABC dating show on Tuesday, July 30.

The engagement ring was a classic oval cut diamond surrounded by a halo of 89 smaller round diamonds to a diamond set band — for a total of 3 karats — designed by Neil Lane. It was handmade in platinum. However, Hannah ditched the ring while confronting Jed about his wrongdoings.

For some background on Hannah’s journey, the Alabama-born beauty queen started with 30 excited Bachelors, but in the end, it came down to two male contenders vying to win Brown’s heart: Tyler Cameron and Jed. There were several hiccups along the way — including many emotional stare-at-the-wall-breaks and the cringiest fantasy suite conversation of all time. But the biggest drama of all? One word: Jed.

The big engagement took place in Greece, after saying a tear-filled goodbye to Tyler before he had the chance to propose. Jed’s proposal, on the other hand, started with a musical number and ended with him proclaiming his love on one knee. Hannah graciously accepted the ring, but in their time together afterwards, she learned via a magazine article that her fiancé was in a serious relationship when he left to film for the Bachelorette.
